“Graveyard of Divine Dragons!”

Tun Ri and the others, who just came, exclaimed. Although they were from the Celestial Longevity Plane, a Graveyard of Divine Dragons was still a very alluring place for them.

“Let’s go!”

Chen Feng waved his hand and a talisman floated up before blowing up with a banging sound, transforming into countless streams of light which entered their bodies. Next, Chen Feng took the lead to fly into the spatial gate.

If I do not display my loyalty now, when will I? Seeing Chen Feng fly forward, Tie Zhen immediately chose to follow. Only, there were others who acted even faster than him. Tun Ri, Tian Wu and Da feng were following Chen Feng already, firmly guarding him while leaving Tie Zhen behind. Tie Zhen dared not say anything about them. At the same time, however, he also understood that some more time was needed for him to be part of Chen Feng’s close circle.

“Young Master, I sense the auras of divine dragons. However, there is danger as well,” Tun Ri said as they were making their way through the spatial gate.

“There is a scent of a scheme here,” Tian Wu said in a sombre tone.

“Not a scheme, a trap.” Da Feng corrected him.

“Isn’t that the same thing?”

“The Graveyard of Divine Dragons will likely not be easy to enter. Most likely, it is the trap that the Six Daos Sword Faction set up. Just be careful. Don’t forget, I am also a disciple of the Six Daos Sword Faction,” Chen Feng said, a clump of golden light shining out from his body. It was a token for the core disciples of the Six Daos Sword Faction that Chen Feng had replicated. The same light was shining out from Tun Ri and the rest. 

“By the way, why didn’t you kill that fire bird earlier?” After not meeting Chen Feng for a million years, Tun Ri had a lot to talk about with Chen Feng.

“No rush.”

“There is also that Longevity Brightness brat. He’s harbouring some sinister thoughts.”

“No rush.” It would appear that Chen Feng was unwilling to talk more about the issue. 

“I have yet to ask you fellows. How was the result of the war back then?” Chen Feng asked after a moment of silence.

“Terrible casualties!” After uttering those two words, Tun Ri grew silent.

After emerging from the spatial passageway, they were greeted by a thick aura of divine dragons. Two massive divine dragons roared as they engaged in a bloodbath. Flowers of blood kept blooming within a red sea of blood. Who knows just how many of the cultivators who entered had died there? Those who were simply dispersed were relatively lucky in comparison.

“As expected, the Six Daos Sword Faction did something to the spatial gate. Those two divine dragons are transformed corpses. The flames around them are black nether fire. Tsk, tsk. They must be the guardians of this Graveyard of Divine Dragons. This is quite the sinister move from the Six Daos Sword Faction. This way, they can turn others into vanguards while depleting their strength. Only, how did the Six Daos Sword Faction enter in the beginning? Could there be a different passageway?” Chen Feng smiled.

“Young Master, I have located Sword Premier Demolisher’s aura. He is not far from here.” Tie Zhen finally stepped forward.

Like Tun Ri’s team, his action of addressing Chen Feng as Young Master represented his attitude towards him in the future. It was unlike the others who addressed Chen Feng as Young Master Longevity Celestial. 

“Let’s go. These two guardians are even stronger than half-step Gold Immortals, albeit slightly. They have spent who knows how long absorbing draconic energy here. They will not be easy to handle,” Chen Feng said. Next, he saw a huge dragon talon – wreathed in black nether fire – swiping towards them to grab them.

“Allow me!” Tun Ri howled and his body became several notches bigger. His head, on the other hand, became several times bigger. Opening his mouth, he revealed blade like teeth and a scarlet-red pillar of light shot out from his mouth to obstruct the dragon talon. 

Bang!

The dragon talon continued swiping forward, dispersing the pillar of light before slapping Tun Ri away. Seeing that, Tian Wu and Da Feng laughed out. At the same time, though, they fired out two blade attacks, which successfully cut down the dragon talon. But when the severed dragon talon fell to the ground, it rapidly disintegrated to ash.

Roar!

Seemingly enraged, one of the divine dragons then smashed its way towards Chen Feng’s group.

“Let’s go!” Da Feng waved his hand and strong winds swept forth to wrap around Chen Feng and the rest before quickly bringing them far away.

Da Feng’s original body was that of a divine bird, endowed with the gift of speed. His move brought everyone along and they fled from the divine dragon. In the end, they managed to shake it off. 

“We’re safe now. That divine dragon is truly formidable. A pity, Lei Long and the others are in cultivation retreat, else he would come running upon finding out that there is a Graveyard of Divine Dragons here.” Tun Ri had recovered. The attack he received earlier had not left any serious wounds on him.

“How is Lei Long and the others?” Chen Feng asked, his voice solemn.

“Lei Long and Wu Jin have already reached the half-step Gold Immortal stage. Sha Gui, Sha Shen and Sha Xian are also in cultivation retreat. As for the others, they were all killed in the war back then. However, some managed to preserve their primary spirits. They are currently still recuperating within the Longevity Pool,” Tun Ri said, his voice turning sombre.

“The casualties back then were so serious?” Chen Feng lamented. He thought back to the tragic battle that happened back then. It was so brutal that even a small child like him was swept into the war. Or perhaps, some of them had simply been targeting him from the very beginning. In the end, only a handful of his bodyguards managed to survive.  

Upon recalling that, Chen Feng’s rage rose once again.

“Tun Ri, what’s the point in saying all that? The most important thing here is that Young Master survived,” Tian Wu shouted coldly.

“Ha ha, that’s right! As long as Young Master is fine, everything is good! We will settle this score in the future!” Tun Ri laughed.

Tun Ri, Tian Wu and Da Feng guarded Chen Feng closely while Tie Zhen and Heavenly Immortal Brightmoon led their subordinates to stay beside them. Heavenly Immortal Longevity Brightness and Prince Fire, on the other hand, were in the rear, their faces dark.

“Sister Brightmoon. It’s been a long time since we last met. You are still as beautiful as ever.” After stopping, Chen Feng smiled and spoke to Heavenly Immortal Brightmoon.

“I’m not worthy of Young Master’s praise.” Heavenly Immortal Brightmoon blushed.

“After so many years, you’ve become so distant.” Chen Feng shook his head.

“Young Master, it is a good thing that we were the first to find you. If those with other motives had found Young Master first, I fear that something bad might have happened,” Heavenly Immortal Brightmoon said, glancing at Prince Fire and Heavenly Immortal Longevity Brightness.

“In recent years, the prestige of the Celestial Longevity Plane has plummeted. There are many in the Immortal Plane who no longer cares for the name Celestial Longevity Plane. And yet, to think that there would still be such a serious infighting.” Chen Feng scoffed.

“The Celestial Longevity Plane is simply too big. There are too many cultivators there. Naturally, various ambitions would grow. Look at the Immortal Court. Even though the Immortal Court is the ruler of the Immortal Plane, even the Immortal Court cannot control the entire Immortal Plane,” Tian Wu said.

“However, the Immortal Court’s strength has grown immensely. It is just a matter of time before they attack the Celestial Planes. Enough. Let’s not talk about this anymore. Hurry up and go find the treasures. We are fortunate enough to be able to find a Graveyard of Divine Dragons,” Chen Feng said. 

“It’s Young Master’s fortune,” everyone quickly said.

Next, Tie Zhen and Heavenly Immortal Brightmoon waved and the ten plus cultivators behind them flew off to disappear in various directions.

“What are you fellows spacing out for? Hurry up and go find treasures for me.” Chen Feng’s tone suddenly turned icy as his gaze turned towards Heavenly Immortal Longevity Brightness and Prince Fire. 

“Affirmative!”

The two swiftly nodded.

“If what you find cannot satisfy me, you’re dead,” Chen Feng continued.

“We will definitely satisfy Young Master!” Longevity Brightness and Prince Fire hastily said.

After the two of them had left, Chen Feng said, “Those two are having improper thoughts. You two, keep an eye out for them.” 

“Don’t worry, Young Master. I have already left an imprint on them both. If they do anything wrong, I will directly head over and kill them off!” Da Feng said with a grin.

“If that’s the case, let’s move out. All the benefits you obtain will belong to you.” After saying that, Chen Feng charged towards a towering mountain.

Covering a very large area, the mountain looked like a tomb from afar. The reason Chen Feng chose this mountain was because he had sensed thick draconic energy from it. He knew that this mountain would surely house the corpses of divine dragons.

Am I so lucky? Chen Feng smiled and sent a fist towards the tomb of the divine dragons.

Boom!

The lands quaked and the massive tomb shook. Next, a stream of draconic energy charged out before transforming into a massive dragon, brandishing its claws and fangs at Chen Feng.

Pure draconic energy blew towards Chen Feng, whose hearted palpitated furiously. This was not something that wyrms could hope to match. In the face of draconic might, lesser cultivators would be killed off by the atmosphere of oppression alone. 

“Heavenly laws. This is a divine dragon at the Heavenly Immortal stage.” Chen Feng did not back away. Instead, he employed the Great Longevity Palm to grab the massive dragon. As for Tun Ri, he stabbed forward with the battle lance in his hand to shatter the massive dragon into streams of draconic energy. Chen Feng then opened his mouth to gulp down the draconic energy. 

“Attack together!”

The semi-transparent silhouette of a divine bird appeared behind Da Feng to blot out the sun. The aura of a divine creature was thus released, almost no different from the aura coming from the tomb.

Although this divine dragon had died, the will to fight left from when it was still alive and the draconic energy stockpiled within the tomb surged forth in a riotous manner to immediately suppress the semi-transparent silhouette that Da Feng released.

At the same time, a massive, semi-transparent silhouette also appeared behind Tian Wu. Tian Wu’s original body was that of a gigantic centipede. Over one hundred Heavenly limbs danced about and 10,000 blades descended upon the tomb in a flash.

Kacha! Kacha! Kacha!

Instantly, countless cracks spread across the tomb before them. Tun Ri and Da Feng began blasting it at the same time and the whole tomb shook.

“Attack!”

Tie Zhen and Heavenly Immortal Brightmoon attacked as well. Finally, the massive tomb collapsed and golden beams of light flew into the distance. Within the golden lights were the treasures that the divine dragons had collected. The treasures were all buried along with the divine dragons after they died. 

“Collect!”

Chen Feng reached his hand out and pressed down, freezing the surrounding space. Then, with a beckoning move from his palm, countless clumps of golden light flew into Chen Feng’s hand. Likewise, Tun Ri and the others were also occupying various positions and they kept taking action. They collected all the clumps of golden light that flew out.

“Although the number of treasures is quite high, their grade is low,” Tun Ri said nonchalantly.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

In the face of their combined attacks, the tomb was finally destroyed. The shell of the tomb tore off to reveal the corpses of over 100 divine dragons. A mighty draconic might spread out and Chen Feng’s group were forced to put some distance between them and the tomb.

“Looks like the size of this Graveyard of Divine Dragons is not small. This is just a tomb that we casually came across. If so, the others will also be able to gain some harvest. Hurry up and find the other tombs. There will surely be corpses of higher-levelled divine dragons.” Chen Feng extended a hand and grasped. The hundred plus corpses of divine dragons were entangled and encased by turbulent spatial powers. In the end, they were all kept inside Chen Feng’s insight acupoints.

Cakar naga terus menggesek ke depan, menyebarkan pilar cahaya sebelum menampar Tun Ri. Melihat itu, Tian Wu dan Da Feng tertawa. Namun, pada saat yang sama, mereka menembakkan dua serangan bilah, yang berhasil memotong cakar naga. Tetapi ketika cakar naga yang terputus jatuh ke tanah, itu dengan cepat hancur menjadi abu.

Mengaum!Tampak marah, salah satu naga ilahi kemudian menghancurkan jalannya menuju kelompok Chen Feng.

Sayang sekali, Lei Long dan yang lainnya sedang dalam retret kultivasi, kalau tidak dia akan berlari saat mengetahui bahwa ada Makam Naga Ilahi di sini. ” Tun Ri telah pulih. Tun Ri, Tian Wu dan Da Feng menjaga Chen Feng dengan ketat sementara Tie Zhen dan Heavenly Immortal Brightmoon memimpin bawahan mereka untuk tetap di samping mereka. Heavenly Immortal Longevity Brightness dan Prince Fire, di sisi lain, ada di belakang, wajah mereka gelap.

“Aku tidak layak mendapat pujian Tuan Muda.” Heavenly Immortal Brightmoon tersipu.

“Setelah bertahun-tahun, kamu menjadi sangat jauh.” Chen Feng menggelengkan kepalanya.

“Tuan Muda, adalah hal yang baik bahwa kami yang pertama kali menemukan Kamu. Jika mereka yang memiliki motif lain telah menemukan Tuan Muda terlebih dahulu, Aku khawatir sesuatu yang buruk mungkin telah terjadi, ”kata Heavenly Immortal Brightmoon, melirik ke arah Pangeran Api dan Kecerahan Panjang Umur Abadi Surgawi.

Mencakup area yang sangat luas, gunung itu tampak seperti kuburan dari jauh. Alasan Chen Feng memilih gunung ini adalah karena dia merasakan energi drakonik yang kental darinya. Dia tahu bahwa gunung ini pasti akan menampung mayat naga dewa.

Apakah Aku sangat beruntung? Chen Feng tersenyum dan mengepalkan tangan ke arah makam naga ilahi.

Ledakan!

Tanah berguncang dan kuburan besar berguncang. Selanjutnya, aliran energi drakonik keluar sebelum berubah menjadi naga besar, mengacungkan cakar dan taringnya ke Chen Feng.

Energi drakonik murni bertiup ke arah Chen Feng, yang jantungnya berdebar kencang. Ini bukanlah sesuatu yang bisa diharapkan oleh wyrms untuk dicocokkan. Dalam menghadapi kekuatan drakonik, pembudidaya yang lebih rendah akan terbunuh oleh atmosfer penindasan saja.

Adapun Tun Ri, dia menusuk ke depan dengan tombak perang di tangannya untuk menghancurkan naga besar itu menjadi aliran energi drakonik. Chen Feng kemudian membuka mulutnya untuk menelan energi drakonik.

“Serang bersama!”

Siluet burung dewa yang semi-transparan muncul di belakang Da Feng untuk menutupi matahari. Aura makhluk ilahi pun dilepaskan, hampir tidak ada bedanya dengan aura yang datang dari kuburan.

Meskipun naga ilahi ini telah mati, keinginan untuk bertarung pergi sejak masih hidup dan energi drakonik yang ditimbun di dalam makam melonjak dengan cara yang riuh untuk segera menekan siluet semi-transparan yang dilepaskan Da Feng.

Pada saat yang sama, siluet besar dan semi-transparan juga muncul di belakang Tian Wu. Tubuh asli Tian Wu adalah kelabang raksasa. Lebih dari seratus anggota tubuh Surgawi menari sekitar dan 10.000 bilah turun ke atas makam dalam sekejap.

Kacha! Kacha! Kacha!

Seketika, retakan yang tak terhitung jumlahnya tersebar di seluruh makam di depan mereka. Tun Ri dan Da Feng mulai meledakkannya pada saat yang sama dan seluruh makam berguncang.

“Menyerang!”

Tie Zhen dan Heavenly Immortal Brightmoon menyerang juga. Akhirnya, makam besar itu runtuh dan berkas cahaya keemasan terbang ke kejauhan. Di dalam cahaya keemasan ada harta karun yang dikumpulkan oleh para naga ilahi. Harta karun itu semua terkubur bersama dengan naga dewa setelah mereka mati.

“Mengumpulkan!”

Chen Feng mengulurkan tangannya dan menekan, membekukan ruang di sekitarnya. Kemudian, dengan gerakan memanggil dari telapak tangannya, gumpalan cahaya keemasan yang tak terhitung jumlahnya terbang ke tangan Chen Feng. Begitu pula Tun Ri dan yang lainnya juga menempati berbagai posisi dan terus melakukan aksi. Mereka mengumpulkan semua gumpalan cahaya keemasan yang terbang keluar.

“Meski jumlah hartanya cukup tinggi, nilainya rendah,” kata Tun Ri acuh tak acuh.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

Dalam menghadapi serangan gabungan mereka, makam itu akhirnya dihancurkan. Cangkang makam robek untuk mengungkapkan mayat lebih dari 100 naga ilahi. Sebuah drakonik perkasa mungkin menyebar dan kelompok Chen Feng dipaksa untuk membuat jarak antara mereka dan makam.

“Sepertinya ukuran Makam Naga Ilahi ini tidak kecil. Ini hanyalah sebuah makam yang kami temui dengan santai. Jika demikian, yang lain juga bisa mendapatkan panen. Cepat dan temukankuburan lainnya. Pasti akan ada mayat Naga Ilahi yang berlevel lebih tinggi. ” Chen Feng mengulurkan tangan dan menggenggam. Ratusan mayat naga ilahi terjerat dan terbungkus oleh kekuatan spasial yang bergejolak. Pada akhirnya, mereka semua disimpan di dalam titik akupuntur wawasan Chen Feng.
