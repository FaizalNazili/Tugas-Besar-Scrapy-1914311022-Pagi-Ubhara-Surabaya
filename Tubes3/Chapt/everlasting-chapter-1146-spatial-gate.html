Due to the retreat of the Starsource Order and Immortal Sword Sect, the 18 spiritual fountains were secured. However, amidst their feelings of delight, Sword Premier Demolisher and the others felt somewhat concerned.

By then, they had guessed Chen Feng’s identity. For him to have six Heavenly Immortals as bodyguards, it was clear that Chen Feng had quite the status in the Celestial Longevity Plane. 

If so, what objectives did Chen Feng have for joining the Six Daos Sword Faction? Was he really after the Six Daos Sword Codex? Given his identity and status, surely, he would not be so obsessed with the Six Daos Sword Codex. And yet, since joining the Six Daos Sword Faction, Chen Feng had not done anything out of the ordinary.

After guessing Chen Feng’s identity, Sword Premier Demolisher felt both worried and flustered. He did not know what to do next.

“Friend Chen, there are some matters that we would like to talk to you about.”

Upon receiving Sword Premier Demolisher’s invitation, Chen Feng smiled.

Judging by the way they addressed him, Chen Feng could already guess how they would treat him next.

“Let’s go. Even though we have yet to even clean up the battlefield, they are already inviting us over. Looks like they are already getting impatient,” Chen Feng said with a chuckle. Due to the way Chen Feng framed the situation, Tie Zhen and the others burst into a smile as well.

“Friend Chen, please sit.” Sword Premier Demolisher and the others showed even greater respect towards Chen Feng. It was not because Chen Feng’s side had rendered assistance earlier, but because of Chen Feng’s identity.

“Seniors, there is no need to stand on ceremony.” Chen Feng sat down while Tie Zhen and his team silently stood behind him. There was not a hint of pretence behind their actions, as though they were only doing something normal. Due to that, Sword Premier Demolisher and the rest grew more certain of Chen Feng’s identity.

This air of a superior – emanating from his very bones – was not something that could be cultivated through extrinsic means.

“I thank Friend Chen for the assistance earlier. Thanks to that, we are able to turn the tide of the battle.” First, Sword Premier Demolisher expressed his thanks for what happened earlier. Just as he was thinking about saying something else, Chen Feng interrupted him.

“Fellows, if you have something to say, then just say it. Let’s just be frank here,” Chen Feng said.

“Very well, we’ll be frank.” Sword Premier Demolisher and the others grew slightly relieved.

“Fellow friend, you must be from the Celestial Longevity Plane, right? The name Chen Qing is also a fake name, no?” Sword Premier Demolisher asked in a serious tone.

“Is this an interrogation?”

“Something like that.”

“You may address me as Chen Feng. I am from the Longevity Clan. The reason I joined the Six Daos Sword Faction is a very simple one, to hide from those who want to kill me.”

“Hide from those who want to kill you?!” Sword Premier Demolisher and the others exchanged glances. What they were worried about had come true.

If Chen Feng was a loose cultivator with a Heaven-defying talent, his existence would be a good thing for the Six Daos Sword Faction. However, by getting dragged into the battles within a behemoth like the Celestial Longevity Plane, the Six Daos Sword Faction would become a cannon fodder.

After some time, Sword Premier Demolisher spoke up again, “I wonder, what do you plan on doing next? The way I see it, a small temple like our Six Daos Sword Faction will be incapable of accommodating Great Gods like you.”

“No wonder the Six Daos Sword Faction would fall. So, the minds and vision of you cultivators are too small.” A slight look of ridicule flashed across Chen Feng’s eyes.

Hearing that, Sword Premier Demolisher and the others became somewhat infuriated. Although Chen Feng had quite the background, he was still just a True Immortal. Level wise, there was still some difference between him and them.

“What? You are all high-level Heavenly Immortals who have been cultivating for at least 10 million years. Are your wills that weak?” The ridiculing look on Chen Feng’s face grew. 

“Chen Feng, are you provoking us? You may be from the Longevity Clan, with an extraordinary background, but this is our Six Daos Sword Faction’s territory. Before this, you even relied on us to avoid your enemies. Do you not know how to be grateful?” Sword Premier Golden Light said.

“If I do not know how to be grateful, I would not have helped out back then. Do you fellows really believed that you could have stopped them just now? Bluntly speaking, if the Starsource Order had not retreated, who knows how many of the Six Daos Sword Faction’s disciples would end up dying. Who knows? Even some of you here would have died. 

“Am I right?

“I’ll make it simple. I still need to stay in the Six Daos Sword Faction for some time. You fellows may choose to not disturb me.”

“That’s not possible. In the previous battle, you fellows have exposed your identities as members of the Longevity Clan. If your pursuers come, wouldn’t that only bring us trouble?” Sword Premier Golden Light said again. 

However, Sword Premier Demolisher interrupted him.

“All right. That is not an issue. However, we also have our own conditions. We need your help for this war.” 

“Not a problem. I will do my best,” Chen Feng said with a smile. 

After the two sides were done with the negotiations, the atmosphere grew somewhat relaxed. Not long after that, Chen Feng left.

“Why did you agree? He is from the Celestial Longevity Plane! We are just an unremarkable little force in the Immortal Plane. We simply have no qualifications to get involved,” Sword Premier Golden Light said with a dissatisfied tone.

“No qualifications? And yet, Chen Feng had still chosen to rely on us, the power of the Six Daos Sword Faction! Besides, we are truly in an unfavourable situation. If we can recruit Chen Feng, we might secure a way out in the future.”

“I’ll send in the report. Also, I will suggest allowing Chen Feng to cultivate the Six Daos Sword Codex.”

“The Six Daos Sword Codex? No! He is just an outsider!”

“Hah! Do you think that he is actually that interested in our Six Daos Sword Codex? The Longevity Scripture is who knows how many more times better than our sect guardian treasure. Not even the long-lost Primordial Scripture of the Six Daos could compare with the Longevity Scripture.”

“The Longevity Scripture! Hey, this Chen Feng probably doesn’t even have the qualifications to cultivate that technique. Hunted down to the point where he has to hide in our sect, he is probably not an important character.”

“Enough! Let’s not talk about this anymore. The most important issue here is to end this war.”

“The way I see it, it will be very difficult, unless we can reach a compromise with them.”

“A compromise? Impossible! The Graveyard of Divine Dragons is the opportunity for our sect to rise again! As long as we can hold them back for just over a thousand years, this Graveyard of Divine Dragons will allow us to regain our Six Daos Sword Faction’s lost glory!”

“As if it’s that simple. Forget a thousand years, even holding them for a hundred years will be impossible. Once news of it spreads out, who knows just how many forces will choose to intervene?”

“At present, we can only hope that the sect can snatch the treasures inside it as fast as possible.”

“Don’t be anxious. Hold on for one more month and it will be our turn.”

“Of course I will be anxious. I am anxious about our current situation. The enemy had only chosen to retreat for now because of Chen Feng. Who knows? They might choose to attack again tomorrow.”

As the high-level Heavenly Immortals were discussing the issue, none of them noticed a strand of divine sense placed nearby. The divine sense was spying on every action and word there.

“As expected, there is indeed a Graveyard of Divine Dragons. Tie Zhen, unexpectedly, your stealth technique is so good.” Images played out before Chen Feng. It was none other than the discussion between Sword Premier Demolisher and the others.

“It’s because their cultivation bases are not high enough,” Tie Zhen said humbly.

“The Graveyard of Divine Dragons. This is the reason why I decided to stay behind. As for the Six Daos Sword Codex, it is nothing in my eyes. If it’s the Primordial Scripture of the Six Daos, though, then maybe.”

“Aren’t there too few of us here?” Tie Zhen said hesitantly.

“Have you contacted the other teams?” Chen Feng asked, smiling.

“I’ve contacted two teams so far.”

“Hmm, are they reliable?” At that very moment, Chen Feng was without the Longevity Tower and the Blood Mustering Bead. Additionally, his two clones were also sharing his power. When compared to the past, he was presently at his weakest state. Should those Heavenly Immortals harbour ulterior motives, they will be hard to handle.

“They are all from the Boundless Corps. They will surely be loyal to the Young Clan Lord!” Tie Zhen could see the concern in Chen Feng’s eyes.

“Let’s do this, then. You go get them to come. We still do not have enough power to snatch the treasures from a Graveyard of Divine Dragons.” Chen Feng finally decided.

“Affirmative!” Tie Zhen nodded.

Regardless of whether the newcomers would be loyal to him or not, at the very least, Tie Zhen’s team of six was completely loyal to him. 

On the other hand, perhaps it was due to the presence of Chen Feng and the others, but the battle over the 18 spiritual fountains stopped completely. The Starsource Order and Immortal Sword Sect had completely moved their forces elsewhere.

Chen Feng, though, requested to stay behind and guard the place. Coincidentally, Sword Premier Demolisher and the others thought of the same thing. Thus, they agreed to Chen Feng’s request. 

Chen Feng had his own reasons for not wanting to leave. Firstly, he did not want to join the war. Secondly, he wanted to absorb the spiritual energy from the 18 spiritual fountains there to cultivate himself. Thirdly, he wanted to secretly keep an eye on Sword Premier Demolisher and the others. 

“When I think about the mysterious Graveyard of Divine Dragons, these spiritual fountains become insignificant.” Chen Feng was seated cross-legged on the ground and an energy wave, formed from 18 streams of spiritual energy, kept flowing into his body. Since recovering his Chaos Constitution, his need for the various types of energy had exceeded the needs of most high-level Heavenly Immortals. 

Tie Zhen’s team, on the other hand, was no longer shocked to see the abnormal display from Chen Feng. As the owner of the Longevity Tower, it was only normal for him to display some abnormalities. However, it did strengthen their resolve to follow Chen Feng.

“Young Master, they’ve begun to move.” As Chen Feng was cultivating, Tie Zhen roused him up. Tie Zhen would never disturb him unless there was something important.

“Let’s go! We’ll follow them!”

Chen Feng knew that Sword Premier Demolisher and the others must be heading towards the Graveyard of Divine Dragons. Unfortunately, the Longevity Tower had yet to return. He could only depend on himself for whatever happened next. That said, Chen Feng was also preparing another force. Only, he was still in the process of contacting this force. He was uncertain if it could arrive in time.

Sword Premier Demolisher and the others were very cautious. They had switched teleportation arrays several times along the way. Due to that, they nearly succeeded in shaking off Chen Feng’s team. When they finally came to a stop, Chen Feng realized that they had returned to the Primordial Mountain Range. It was a territory under the Six Daos Sword Faction’s control.

“Could the entrance to the Graveyard of Divine Dragons be located here?” Chen Feng felt puzzled. He had spent the past few years roaming around the Primordial Mountain Range. He had also come to this valley before, but he had not found anything special about this place.

“There are no abnormalities here,” Tie Zhen said as well.

It was then that the space there shook, like ripples of water. Next, a mirror, created by the massive water ripples, appeared in the valley. Only, following the emergence of the mirror’s frame, Chen Feng knew that it was not actually a mirror. Rather, it was a mysterious spatial gate.

“So, it is a spatial gate. If so, not even taking down the Six Daos Sword Faction would help the other party. Not unless they are able to obtain this spatial gate.” Chen Feng nodded. At the same time, he began considering his next move. Without the assistance of the Longevity Tower, it would not be easy for his team to secretly enter.

Senior, tidak perlu berdiri di atas upacara. Chen Feng duduk sementara Tie Zhen dan timnya diam-diam berdiri di belakangnya. Tidak ada tanda-tanda kepura-puraan di balik tindakan mereka, seolah-olah mereka hanya melakukan sesuatu yang normal. “Aku berterima kasih kepada Teman Chen atas bantuannya sebelumnya. Berkat itu, kami dapat mengubah gelombang pertempuran. ” Pertama, Sword Premier Demolisher mengucapkan terima kasih atas apa yang terjadi sebelumnya. Saat dia berpikir untuk mengatakan sesuatu yang lain, Chen Feng memotongnya.

“Teman-teman, jika kamu ingin mengatakan sesuatu, katakan saja. Jujur saja di sini, “kata Chen Feng.

Baiklah, kami akan jujur. Sword Premier Demolisher dan yang lainnya menjadi sedikit lega.

Jika Chen Feng adalah seorang kultivator lepas dengan bakat menantang Surga, keberadaannya akan menjadi hal yang baik untuk Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao. Namun, dengan terseret ke dalam pertempuran di dalam raksasa seperti Pesawat Panjang Umur Surga, Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao akan menjadi umpan meriam.

Setelah beberapa waktu, Sword Premier Demolisher berbicara lagi, “Aku ingin tahu, apa yang akan kamu lakukan selanjutnya? Menurutku, kuil kecil seperti Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao kami tidak akan mampu menampung Dewa Agung sepertimu. ”

“Apa? Kamu semua Dewa Surgawi tingkat tinggi yang telah berkultivasi setidaknya selama 10 juta tahun. Apakah keinginanmu itu lemah? ” Ekspresi mengejek di wajah Chen Feng tumbuh.

“Chen Feng, apakah kamu memprovokasi kami? Kamu mungkin dari Klan Panjang Umur, dengan latar belakang yang luar biasa, tapi ini adalah wilayah Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao kami. Sebelum ini, Kamu bahkan mengandalkan kami untuk menghindari musuh Kamu. Apakah kamu tidak tahu bagaimana bersyukur? ” Kata Pedang Premier Golden Light.

Terus terang, jika Starsource Order tidak mundur, siapa yang tahu berapa banyak murid dari Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao yang akan mati. Siapa tahu? Bahkan beberapa dari Kamu di sini akan mati.

“Apakah Aku benar?

Aku akan membuatnya sederhana. Aku masih harus tinggal di Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao untuk beberapa waktu. Kalian mungkin memilih untuk tidak menggangguku. ”

“Itu tidak mungkin. Di pertempuran sebelumnya, kalian telah mengungkap identitas kalian sebagai anggota Klan Panjang Umur. Jika pengejarmu datang, bukankah itu hanya membawa masalah bagi kami? ” Pedang Premier Golden Light berkata lagi.

Namun, Sword Premier Demolisher menyela dia.

“Baiklah. Itu bukan masalah. Namun, kami juga punya syarat sendiri. Kami membutuhkan bantuan Kamu untuk perang ini. ”

“Bukan masalah. Aku akan melakukan yang terbaik, ”kata Chen Feng sambil tersenyum.

Setelah kedua belah pihak selesai bernegosiasi, suasana menjadi agak santai. Tidak lama setelah itu, Chen Feng pergi.

“Mengapa Kamu setuju? Dia berasal dari Pesawat Panjang Umur Surga! Kami hanyalah kekuatan kecil yang biasa-biasa saja di Immortal Plane. Kami hanya tidak memiliki kualifikasi untuk terlibat, ”kata Sword Premier Golden Light dengan nada tidak puas.

“Tidak ada kualifikasi? Namun, Chen Feng masih memilih untuk mengandalkan kami, kekuatan Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao! Selain itu, kami benar-benar berada dalam situasi yang tidak menguntungkan. Jika kami dapat merekrut Chen Feng, kami mungkin akan mendapatkan jalan keluar di masa depan. ”

Aku akan mengirimkan laporannya. Juga, Aku akan menyarankan agar Chen Feng mengolah Kodeks Pedang Enam Tao. ”

“Kodeks Pedang Enam Tao? Tidak! Dia hanya orang luar! ”

“Hah! Apakah Kamu pikir dia benar-benar tertarik dengan Six Daos Sword Codex kita? The Longevity Scripture adalah siapa yang tahu berapa kali lebih baik dari harta penjaga sekte kita. Bahkan Primordial Scripture of the Six Tao yang telah lama hilang tidak bisa dibandingkan dengan Longevity Scripture. ”

“Kitab Suci Umur Panjang! Hei, Chen Feng ini mungkin bahkan tidak memiliki kualifikasi untuk mengembangkan teknik itu. Diburu sampai ke titik di mana dia harus bersembunyi di sekte kita, dia mungkin bukan karakter penting. ”

“Cukup! Jangan bicarakan ini lagi. Masalah terpenting di sini adalah mengakhiri perang ini. ”

“Menurut pandangan Aku, itu akan sangat sulit, kecuali kita bisa mencapai kompromi dengan mereka.”

“Kompromi? Mustahil! Makam Naga Ilahi adalah kesempatan bagi sekte kita untuk bangkit kembali! Selama kita bisa menahan mereka selama lebih dari seribu tahun, Makam Naga Ilahi ini akan memungkinkan kita untuk mendapatkan kembali kejayaan Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao kita yang hilang! ”

“Seolah-olah sesederhana itu. Lupakan seribu tahun, bahkan menahannya selama seratus tahun tidaklah mungkin. Setelah berita itu menyebar, siapa yang tahu berapa banyak kekuatan yang akan memilih untuk campur tangan? ”

“Saat ini, kami hanya bisa berharap sekte tersebut dapat merebut harta karun di dalamnya secepat mungkin.”

“Jangan cemas. Tunggu selama satu bulan lagi dan sekarang giliran kita. ”

“Tentu saja Aku akan gelisah. Aku cemas tentang situasi kita saat ini. Musuh hanya memilih mundur untuk saat ini karena Chen Feng. Siapa tahu? Mereka mungkin memilih untuk menyerang lagi besok. ”

Saat Dewa Surgawi tingkat tinggi sedang mendiskusikan masalah ini, tidak ada dari mereka yang memperhatikan seutas perasaan ilahi ditempatkan di dekatnya. Perasaan ilahi memata-matai setiap tindakan dan kata di sana.

“Seperti yang diharapkan, memang ada Makam Naga Ilahi. Tie Zhen, tanpa diduga, teknik silumanmu sangat bagus. ” Gambar diputar sebelum Chen Feng. Itu tidak lain adalah diskusi antara Sword Premier Demolisher dan yang lainnya.

“Itu karena basis kultivasi mereka tidak cukup tinggi,” kata Tie Zhen dengan rendah hati.

“Kuburan Naga Ilahi. Inilah alasan mengapa Aku memutuskan untuk tetap tinggal. Adapun Six Daos Sword Codex, tidak ada apa-apa di mata Aku. Jikaitu adalah Primordial Scripture of the Six Tao, lalu mungkin. ”

“Bukankah kita terlalu sedikit di sini?” Tie Zhen berkata dengan ragu-ragu.

“Sudahkah Kamu menghubungi tim lain?” Chen Feng bertanya sambil tersenyum.

Aku telah menghubungi dua tim sejauh ini.

“Hmm, apakah mereka dapat diandalkan?” Pada saat itu, Chen Feng tanpa Menara Umur Panjang dan Manik Pengumpul Darah. Selain itu, kedua klonnya juga berbagi kekuatannya. Jika dibandingkan dengan masa lalu, dia saat ini berada pada kondisi terlemahnya. Jika Dewa Surgawi itu menyimpan motif tersembunyi, mereka akan sulit ditangani.

“Mereka semua dari Korps Tanpa Batas. Mereka pasti akan setia kepada Tuan Klan Muda! ” Tie Zhen bisa melihat kekhawatiran di mata Chen Feng.

“Ayo lakukan ini, kalau begitu. Dapatkan mereka untuk datang. Kami masih belum memiliki kekuatan yang cukup untuk merebut harta karun dari Makam Naga Ilahi. ” Chen Feng akhirnya memutuskan.

“Setuju!” Tie Zhen mengangguk.

Terlepas dari apakah para pendatang baru akan setia padanya atau tidak, paling tidak, tim enam orang Tie Zhen benar-benar setia padanya.

Di sisi lain, mungkin itu karena kehadiran Chen Feng dan yang lainnya, tetapi pertempuran memperebutkan 18 air mancur spiritual berhenti sama sekali. The Starsource Order dan Immortal Sword Sect telah sepenuhnya memindahkan pasukan mereka ke tempat lain.

Chen Feng, bagaimanapun, meminta untuk tetap tinggal dan menjaga tempat itu. Secara kebetulan, Sword Premier Demolisher dan yang lainnya memikirkan hal yang sama. Jadi, mereka menyetujui permintaan Chen Feng.

Chen Feng punya alasan sendiri untuk tidak ingin pergi. Pertama, dia tidak mau ikut perang. Kedua, dia ingin menyerap energi spiritual dari 18 air mancur spiritual di sana untuk mengolah dirinya sendiri. Ketiga, dia ingin diam-diam mengawasi Sword Premier Demolisher dan yang lainnya.

“Saat aku memikirkan tentang Makam Naga Ilahi yang misterius, air mancur spiritual ini menjadi tidak berarti.” Chen Feng duduk bersila di tanah dan gelombang energi, yang terbentuk dari 18 aliran energi spiritual, terus mengalir ke tubuhnya. Sejak memulihkan Konstitusi Chaos-nya, kebutuhannya akan berbagai jenis energi telah melebihi kebutuhan Dewa Surgawi tingkat tinggi.

Tim Tie Zhen, di sisi lain, tidak lagi terkejut melihat tampilan abnormal dari Chen Feng. Sebagai pemilik Menara Umur Panjang, itu normal baginya untuk menunjukkan beberapa ketidaknormalan. Namun, itu memperkuat tekad mereka untuk mengikuti Chen Feng.

Tuan Muda, mereka sudah mulai bergerak. Saat Chen Feng sedang berkultivasi, Tie Zhen membangunkannya. Tie Zhen tidak akan mengganggunya kecuali ada sesuatu yang penting.

“Ayo pergi! Kami akan mengikuti mereka! ”

Chen Feng tahu bahwa Sword Premier Demolisher dan yang lainnya pasti menuju Makam Naga Ilahi. Sayangnya, Menara Umur Panjang belum kembali. Dia hanya bisa bergantung pada dirinya sendiri untuk apa pun yang terjadi selanjutnya. Konon, Chen Feng juga sedang mempersiapkan kekuatan lain. Hanya saja, dia masih dalam proses menghubungi pasukan ini. Dia tidak yakin apakah itu bisa tiba tepat waktu.

Sword Premier Demolisher dan yang lainnya sangat berhati-hati. Mereka telah mengganti susunan teleportasi beberapa kali di sepanjang jalan. Karena itu, mereka hampir berhasil menyingkirkan tim Chen Feng. Ketika mereka akhirnya berhenti, Chen Feng menyadari bahwa mereka telah kembali ke Pegunungan Primordial. Itu adalah wilayah di bawah kendali Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao.

“Mungkinkah pintu masuk ke Makam Naga Ilahi terletak di sini?” Chen Feng merasa bingung. Dia telah menghabiskan beberapa tahun terakhir berkeliaran di sekitar Pegunungan Primordial. Dia juga pernah datang ke lembah ini sebelumnya, tetapi dia belum menemukan sesuatu yang istimewa tentang tempat ini.

“Tidak ada kelainan di sini,” kata Tie Zhen juga.

Saat itulah ruang di sana bergetar, seperti riak air. Selanjutnya, sebuah cermin, yang diciptakan oleh riak air yang sangat besar, muncul di lembah. Hanya saja, setelah munculnya bingkai cermin tersebut, Chen Feng tahu bahwa itu sebenarnya bukanlah cermin. Sebaliknya, itu adalah gerbang spasial yang misterius.

“Jadi, itu adalah gerbang spasial. Jika demikian, bahkan tidak menjatuhkan Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao akan membantu pihak lain. Tidak, kecuali mereka bisa mendapatkan gerbang spasial ini. ” Chen Feng mengangguk. Pada saat yang sama, dia mulai mempertimbangkan langkah selanjutnya. Tanpa bantuan Menara Umur Panjang, tidak akan mudah bagi timnya untuk masuk secara diam-diam.
