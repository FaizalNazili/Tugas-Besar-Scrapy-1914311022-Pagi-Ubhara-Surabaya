Chapter 484: Secret Meeting

Translator: EndlessFantasy Translation  Editor: EndlessFantasy Translation

“How’s that possible…”

Hearing Nancy’s words, many people once again looked in Yu Qi’s direction and Yu Qi was even more stunned. “That’s impossible. The Quantum Laboratory is the top secret of the Earth Federation. There are only a few thousand higher-ups who know of the lab, which is surrounded by a hundred miles of uninhabited region. How could the legal owner of Blacklight Biotechnology appear there?”

“That’s for China to answer.”

Nancy snorted. “No one knows what happened but the witness did see him. The witness was a shield bearer from Northern America who had just resigned. He was originally going to act as a technical consultant and hand over the position to a new shield bearer. When the disaster happened, he quickly hid in the quantum core area and survived, becoming one of the few survivors in the laboratory.”

Hearing the argument between the two, Kirsty, as the chairman of this month, suddenly looked as if he had a headache. Due to political reasons, he mostly had to maintain the same stance as Northern America. However, this event was too unbelievable which made things difficult for him.

Even so, just as the venue was becoming noisy, there was a knock on the door outside the venue. Kirsty immediately picked up the gavel and banged on the table to make everyone quiet. Then, he saw a soldier in the uniform of the United Front coming in.

As soon as the soldier walked in, he went straight to the rostrum, and then he said something into Kirsty’s ears. Kirsty looked at the soldier with a look of astonishment when he heard the words and then looked outside the door again. Then he said, “Today’s emergency meeting has temporarily ended. Alright, meeting adjourned!”

With that, he banged the gavel again, shot a meaningful glance at Nancy, then turned hurriedly and left the venue.

Seeing Kirsty suddenly leave, everyone was even more astounded. For a while, the place was in chaos. Many people gathered together and discussed in whispers the precious information that they had just heard.

Seeing that Kirsty had left in a hurry, Nancy seemed to think of something. He glanced at Yu Qi who was opposite, then followed Kirsty out and disappeared outside the door of the conference room.

Seeing these two people leave in such a sneaky manner, Yu Qi’s expression was stormy and uncertain. After all, before he was suddenly attacked, his superiors had given him no warning and he was completely ignorant of all this.

Moreover, although the various continents of the Earth Federation were frequently in lively conflict, in the United Front, there were not many instances of slander or defamation. Even if they had wanted to make a scapegoat out of China, they would not have picked such a lousy story.

Did this mean that Blacklight Biotechnology was involved in this matter?

Thinking of this, Yu Qi’s heart also trembled in shock.

He was secretly rather opposed to the sudden rise of Blacklight Biotechnology. As a politician, he had the natural instinct of every politician, which was to control everything in his hands and maintain stability, without any unexpected situations.

Civilization needed stability, thus the Earth Federation existed.

The country needed stability, thus the army and nuclear weapons existed.

The people needed stability, thus the law existed…

However, the existence of Blacklight Biotechnology had broken this stability.

All the continents were well aware of what this company had done in Africa — starting from the construction of the nuclear power plant four years ago. Then, based on the unique advantages brought by the nuclear power plant, an entire city was built. At the same time, the company vigorously developed the industry system, tying the entire country’s population to its own chariot of interests.

It took the people of the entire country as hostages and bit by bit turned the government of this country into a mere figurehead.

By now, how many rights did Namibia have to itself?

In addition, looting all kinds of rare resources in Africa in the form of transactions was also the company’s specialty. It even secretly subsidized the rebels to launch a coup to obtain germanium mines from a small country until the germanium mines had completely fallen into its pockets.

Originally, this kind of company should have been dismantled by the Earth Federation’s sanctions a long time ago and its legal owner should be sent to the Federal International Court of Justice. Nonetheless, this company had enticed countless European and American consortia into joining its forces with its ultra-advanced biotechnology, instantly turning the enemy into an ally, eliminating 80% of the resistance.

Strictly speaking, these tactics were not that outstanding. It was not that other companies had never thought of doing all this, but those companies were a long way from the level of Blacklight Biotechnology in terms of technology.

As early as the beginning of this year, the Earth Federation had analyzed the biotechnology of Blacklight Biotechnology. Many experts believed that the level of biotechnology currently possessed by Blacklight Biotechnology had exceeded that of the Earth Federation by more than 50 years…

Blacklight Biotechnology had raised the level of biotechnology in the Earth Federation and even caused the entire human society to feel the impact of their biotechnology.

However, this was a beneficial thing for the entire humankind after all. Governments of all countries could only put up with this, half in agony, half in delight. In addition, the company was not monopolizing these technologies but was willing to sell them to all continents, which was why Blacklight Biotechnology could keep growing until it became the mammoth it was today.

Otherwise, the Earth Federation would simply charge this company with illegal monopoly or war crimes, which would force it to face the consequences of its actions.

Even so, later, this company acted fishier. First, the company suddenly broke out of its field and entered the electronics industry and even unveiled the black technology of holographic projection and brainwave control. After that, the gloves were off. The company began to get involved in the military industry and aerospace.

At this point, the Northern American Continent finally could not sit still and began planning to create conflicts to act against Blacklight Biotechnology.

What happened next was known to everyone. Blacklight Biotechnology was suspected of controlling a certain secret weapon, which once disrupted the normal social order in Northern America and eventually caused society to stop moving.

At this moment, a thought occurred to Yu Qi — everyone here was responsible for how powerful Blacklight Biotechnology had become.

He shook his head, walked out of the United Front conference room behind everyone else, and retrieved his mobile phone at the entrance.

Then he saw that the notification light of his mobile phone was green.

He instinctively turned on the phone and as expected, there was a caller ID, but when he saw the label of the caller ID, he felt a quick jolt.

The one who called him turned out to be his immediate superior!

Although Yu Qi was a representative of China in the Earth Federation United Front, he was not the top leader of China in this department. He was not even in the top three in command. He was equivalent to a spokesperson. The group’s direction was determined by the big shot behind all of them.

Without thinking, Yu Qi cleared his throat quickly and then immediately pressed the callback button.

The call was swiftly connected after only a brief beeping sound.

“Secretary Lin, were you looking for me?”

Yu Qi returned to using Chinese and spoke in clear syllables, “I was about to report to you that a major event has occurred in the Quantum Deity Laboratory, which is suspected to be related to the company Blacklight Biotechnology…”

“I already know.”

On the phone, the other party’s voice calmly said, “Now, head out of the conference room and come meet me in front of Door C in Hall 1.”

“Ah, Secretary Lin, have you come to the Earth Federation headquarters?”

Yu Qi was immediately stunned. It was no ordinary matter for Secretary Lin, who had always been so busy, to come to the Earth Federation headquarters in Switzerland. After all, as a leader at the continental level, every move would arouse endless suspicion. Moreover, the security of these leading figures was of utmost priority. Everything they did would cause a ripple effect.

Still, in the next second, Yu Qi knew that he had asked the wrong question. He did not wait for the other person to answer and said quickly, “I understand, I’ll come over right away!”

Then, Yu Qi hung up the phone.

He kept walking toward Hall 1, but he saw Nancy and Kirsty also walking in front of him. Based on their hurried footsteps, they seemed to be preparing to meet some big shots as well.

Yu Qi was even more puzzled. He followed them to Hall 1. However, at this moment, Nancy and Kirsty suddenly separated in front of him and walked in the direction of Door A and Door B respectively.

Those two directions were also the entrances of Hall 1, just like the terminals of an airport. To better receive travelers, the airport had multiple terminals to welcome passengers from all over the world and the same was true with Hall 1. To welcome the leaders of various countries and prevent two parties from meeting awkwardly at the entrance, every time the higher-ups of the countries arrived at the Earth Federation, they would be diverted through various entrances.

Seeing this scene, Yu Qi was stunned. Sure enough, not only his immediate superior but also the big bosses of Nancy and Kirsty had come…

It seemed that the Earth Federation was going through something extraordinary…

Thinking of this, Yu Qi no longer paid attention to the whereabouts of the two. He directly followed the route to the entrance of Door C.

Suddenly, Yu Qi saw a group of guards with real swords and guns walking out of a car, surrounding his immediate superior.

“Sec, Secretary Lin!”

Yu Qi hurriedly rushed forward, about to shake hands with the other man, but the old man called Secretary Lin just waved his hand and at the same time stepped aside.

At this point, Yu Qi realized that there was another person behind Secretary Lin!

He was a refined-looking man, about sixty years old, with swept-back hair that was meticulously combed. At this time, he was smiling and walking toward Yu Qi.

When Yu Qi saw this man’s face clearly, his head suddenly buzzed and his brain totally crashed!

“Comrade Little Yu, thank you for your hard work here.”

Seeing Yu Qi’s expression, the man could not help but smile lightly and at the same time, stretched out his hand toward Yu Qi.

Yu Qi was an elite after all. After seeing the man speaking to him, he immediately recovered from his daze. He grasped the man’s hand with both hands and said excitedly, “It’s not hard, it’s not hard at all!”

Seeing this, the man smiled kindly and then walked toward the inside of the hall, surrounded by guards.

“Little Yu, let’s go too.”

Behind him, Secretary Lin patted Yu Qi on the shoulder and exhorted, “You’re our representative in the Earth Federation United Front. You have many years of diplomatic experience and are outstanding in terms of work ability. Therefore, the leader called for you to participate in this important meeting.”

While speaking, Secretary Lin secretly warned, “Remember, talk less and observe more at the meeting. You must remember every detail in the meeting. This is very important to us, understand?”

“Yes!”

Seeing this, Yu Qi naturally had no choice of shirking his duties. He nodded repeatedly. After Secretary Lin stepped forward, he followed behind at an appropriate distance.

Being able to participate in such a major conference was an unforgettable, once-in-a-lifetime opportunity, an opportunity to continue climbing up and even enter the core of power in China!

Thinking of this, Yu Qi only felt a surge in his heart. He quickly took a deep breath and followed everyone into an elevator.

However, Yu Qi looked at the buttons in the elevator but found that no one had pressed the buttons all the while. He could not help but look at his immediate superior in confusion, but the other party seemed to know what he was thinking and only shook his head silently.

About ten seconds later, the elevator doors closed with a “ding”.

Yu Qi was wondering why no one had pressed any buttons in the elevator when he suddenly felt a sense of weightlessness. He subconsciously looked at the elevator display, only to find that the screen display was going by negative numbers!

In other words, the elevator was not heading upward at this time, but was going downward!

What, after having stayed here for so long, he did not even know that there were underground facilities in the Earth Federation headquarters?

Yu Qi was extremely surprised but when the elevator arrived at its floor, he recalled something when he saw Secretary Lin stepping out of the elevator first and skillfully guiding the elegant man forward.

Secretary Lin used to be a spokesperson like Yu Qi was. It was after several years of training in this position in the United Front that he finally got on top and became the present Secretary Lin…

Melihat Kirsty tiba-tiba pergi, semua orang menjadi lebih tercengang. Untuk sementara, tempat itu kacau balau. Banyak orang berkumpul dan berdiskusi dengan berbisik tentang informasi berharga yang baru saja mereka dengar.

Melihat Kirsty pergi dengan tergesa-gesa, Nancy sepertinya memikirkan sesuatu. Dia melirik Yu Qi yang berlawanan, lalu mengikuti Kirsty keluar dan menghilang di luar pintu ruang konferensi.

Melihat kedua orang ini pergi dengan cara yang licik, ekspresi Yu Qi menjadi badai dan tidak pasti. Bagaimanapun, sebelum dia tiba-tiba diserang, atasannya tidak memberinya peringatan dan dia sama sekali tidak tahu tentang semua ini.

Apalagi, meski berbagai benua di Federasi Bumi sering mengalami konflik yang meriah, di Front Bersatu, tidak banyak contoh fitnah atau pencemaran nama baik. Bahkan jika mereka ingin membuat kambing hitam dari China, mereka tidak akan memilih cerita yang buruk.

Apakah ini berarti Blacklight Biotechnology terlibat dalam masalah ini?

Memikirkan hal ini, hati Yu Qi juga bergetar karena terkejut.

Dia diam-diam agak menentang munculnya Blacklight Biotechnology. Sebagai seorang politikus, ia memiliki naluri alami dari setiap politikus, yaitu mengontrol segala sesuatu yang ada di tangannya dan menjaga stabilitas, tanpa situasi yang tidak terduga.

Peradaban membutuhkan stabilitas, sehingga Federasi Bumi ada.

Negara membutuhkan stabilitas, oleh karena itu tentara dan senjata nuklir ada.

Orang-orang membutuhkan stabilitas, oleh karena itu hukum itu ada …

Namun, keberadaan Blacklight Biotechnology telah merusak stabilitas ini.

Semua benua sangat menyadari apa yang telah dilakukan perusahaan ini di Afrika – mulai dari pembangunan pembangkit listrik tenaga nuklir empat tahun lalu. Kemudian, berdasarkan keunggulan unik yang dibawa oleh pembangkit listrik tenaga nuklir, seluruh kota dibangun. Pada saat yang sama, perusahaan dengan penuh semangat mengembangkan sistem industri, mengikat seluruh penduduk negara dengan kereta kepentingannya sendiri.

Itu mengambil orang-orang dari seluruh negeri sebagai sandera dan sedikit demi sedikit mengubah pemerintah negara ini menjadi boneka belaka.

Sekarang, berapa banyak hak yang dimiliki Namibia untuk dirinya sendiri?

Selain itu, menjarah semua jenis sumber daya langka di Afrika dalam bentuk transaksi juga menjadi keahlian perusahaan. Ia bahkan diam-diam mensubsidi para pemberontak untuk meluncurkan akudeta untuk mendapatkan tambang germanium dari sebuah negara kecil sampai tambang germanium benar-benar jatuh ke kantongnya.

Awalnya, perusahaan semacam ini seharusnya sudah lama dibongkar oleh sanksi Federasi Bumi dan pemilik sahnya harus dikirim ke Pengadilan Internasional Federal. Meskipun demikian, perusahaan ini telah membujuk konsorsium Eropa dan Amerika yang tak terhitung jumlahnya untuk bergabung dengan kekuatannya dengan bioteknologi yang sangat canggih, langsung mengubah musuh menjadi sekutu, menghilangkan 80% perlawanan.

Sebenarnya, taktik ini tidak terlalu menonjol. Bukannya perusahaan lain tidak pernah berpikir untuk melakukan semua ini, tetapi perusahaan-perusahaan itu jauh dari level Blacklight Biotechnology dalam hal teknologi.

Pada awal tahun ini, Federasi Bumi telah menganalisis bioteknologi Bioteknologi Blacklight. Banyak ahli percaya bahwa tingkat bioteknologi yang saat ini dimiliki oleh Blacklight Biotechnology telah melebihi tingkat Federasi Bumi selama lebih dari 50 tahun …

Blacklight Biotechnology telah meningkatkan level bioteknologi di Federasi Bumi dan bahkan menyebabkan seluruh masyarakat manusia merasakan dampak dari bioteknologi mereka.

Namun, bagaimanapun, ini adalah hal yang bermanfaat bagi seluruh umat manusia. Pemerintah dari semua negara hanya bisa tahan dengan ini, setengah menderita, setengah senang. Selain itu, perusahaan tidak memonopoli teknologi ini tetapi bersedia menjualnya ke semua benua, itulah sebabnya Blacklight Biotechnology dapat terus berkembang hingga menjadi raksasa seperti sekarang ini.

Jika tidak, Federasi Bumi hanya akan menuntut perusahaan ini dengan monopoli ilegal atau kejahatan perang, yang akan memaksanya untuk menghadapi konsekuensi dari tindakannya.

Meski begitu, belakangan, perusahaan ini bertindak lebih mencurigakan. Pertama, perusahaan tiba-tiba keluar dari bidangnya dan memasuki industri elektronik dan bahkan meluncurkan teknologi hitam proyeksi holografik dan kontrol gelombang otak. Setelah itu, sarung tangan dilepas. Perusahaan mulai terlibat dalam industri militer dan kedirgantaraan.

Pada titik ini, Benua Amerika Utara akhirnya tidak bisa diam dan mulai merencanakan untuk menciptakan konflik untuk melawan Blacklight Biotechnology.

Apa yang terjadi selanjutnya diketahui semua orang. Blacklight Biotechnology diduga mengendalikan senjata rahasia tertentu, yang pernah mengganggu tatanan sosial normal di Amerika Utara dan akhirnya menyebabkan masyarakat berhenti bergerak.

Pada saat ini, sebuah pemikiran muncul di Yu Qi – semua orang di sini bertanggung jawab atas betapa kuatnya Bioteknologi Blacklight.

Dia menggelengkan kepalanya, berjalan keluar dari ruang konferensi United Front di belakang semua orang, dan mengambil ponselnya di pintu masuk.

Kemudian dia melihat bahwa lampu notifikasi ponselnya berwarna hijau.

Dia secara naluriah menyalakan telepon dan seperti yang diharapkan, ada ID penelepon, tetapi ketika dia melihat label ID penelepon, dia merasakan sentakan cepat.

Orang yang memanggilnya ternyata adalah atasan langsungnya!

Meskipun Yu Qi adalah perwakilan China di Front Persatuan Federasi Bumi, dia bukanlah pemimpin tertinggi China di departemen ini. Dia bahkan tidak berada di posisi tiga teratas dalam komando. Dia setara dengan seorang juru bicara. Arah grup ditentukan oleh tembakan besar di belakang mereka semua.

Tanpa pikir panjang, Yu Qi berdehem dengan cepat lalu segera menekan tombol callback.

Panggilan itu dengan cepat terhubung setelah hanya terdengar bunyi bip singkat.

“Sekretaris Lin, apakah Kamu mencari Aku?”

Yu Qi kembali menggunakan bahasa China dan berbicara dengan suku kata yang jelas, “Aku akan melaporkan kepada Kamu bahwa peristiwa besar telah terjadi di Laboratorium Dewa Kuantum, yang diduga terkait dengan perusahaan Blacklight Biotechnology …”

“Aku sudah tahu.”

Di telepon, suara pihak lain dengan tenang berkata, “Sekarang, keluarlah dari ruang konferensi dan temui Aku di depan Pintu C di Aula 1.”

“Ah, Sekretaris Lin, apakah Kamu sudah datang ke markas Federasi Bumi?”

Yu Qi langsung tertegun. Bukan masalah biasa bagi Sekretaris Lin, yang selalu sibuk, datang ke markas Federasi Bumi di Swiss. Bagaimanapun, sebagai pemimpin di tingkat kontinental, setiap gerakan akan menimbulkan kecurigaan yang tak ada habisnya. Selain itu, keamanan tokoh-tokoh terkemuka ini menjadi prioritas utama. Semua yang mereka lakukan akan menimbulkan efek riak.

Namun, di detik berikutnya, Yu Qi tahu bahwa dia telah menanyakan pertanyaan yang salah. Dia tidak menunggu p lainnyaSeseorang untuk menjawab dan berkata dengan cepat, “Aku mengerti, Aku akan segera datang!”

Kemudian, Yu Qi menutup telepon.

Dia terus berjalan menuju Aula 1, tetapi dia melihat Nancy dan Kirsty juga berjalan di depannya. Berdasarkan langkah kaki mereka yang tergesa-gesa, mereka tampaknya bersiap untuk menghadapi beberapa pukulan besar juga.

Yu Qi bahkan lebih bingung. Dia mengikuti mereka ke Aula 1. Namun, pada saat ini, Nancy dan Kirsty tiba-tiba berpisah di depannya dan berjalan ke arah Pintu A dan Pintu B.

Kedua arah itu juga merupakan pintu masuk Hall 1, persis seperti terminal bandara. Untuk menerima wisatawan dengan lebih baik, bandara memiliki banyak terminal untuk menyambut penumpang dari seluruh dunia dan hal yang sama berlaku untuk Hall 1. Untuk menyambut para pemimpin dari berbagai negara dan mencegah dua pihak untuk bertemu dengan canggung di pintu masuk, setiap kali lebih tinggi- Setelah negara-negara tiba di Federasi Bumi, mereka akan dialihkan melalui berbagai pintu masuk.

Melihat adegan ini, Yu Qi tertegun. Benar saja, tidak hanya atasan langsungnya tetapi juga bos besar Nancy dan Kirsty telah datang…

Tampaknya Federasi Bumi sedang mengalami sesuatu yang luar biasa …

Memikirkan hal ini, Yu Qi tidak lagi memperhatikan keberadaan keduanya. Dia langsung mengikuti rute ke pintu masuk Pintu C.

Tiba-tiba, Yu Qi melihat sekelompok penjaga dengan pedang dan senjata sungguhan berjalan keluar dari mobil, mengelilingi atasan langsungnya.

“Sec, Sekretaris Lin!”

Yu Qi buru-buru bergegas maju, hendak berjabat tangan dengan pria lain, tetapi pria tua bernama Sekretaris Lin hanya melambaikan tangannya dan pada saat yang sama melangkah ke samping.

Pada titik ini, Yu Qi menyadari bahwa ada orang lain di belakang Sekretaris Lin!

Dia adalah seorang pria berpenampilan halus, sekitar enam puluh tahun, dengan rambut disisir ke belakang yang disisir dengan cermat. Saat ini, dia tersenyum dan berjalan menuju Yu Qi.

Ketika Yu Qi melihat wajah pria ini dengan jelas, kepalanya tiba-tiba berdengung dan otaknya benar-benar hancur!

“Kamerad Yu Kecil, terima kasih atas kerja kerasmu di sini.”

Melihat ekspresi Yu Qi, pria itu tidak bisa menahan senyum ringan dan pada saat yang sama, mengulurkan tangannya ke arah Yu Qi.

Yu Qi adalah seorang elit. Setelah melihat pria itu berbicara dengannya, dia segera pulih dari linglung. Dia menggenggam tangan pria itu dengan kedua tangannya dan berkata dengan penuh semangat, “Tidak sulit, tidak sulit sama sekali!”

Melihat ini, pria itu tersenyum ramah dan kemudian berjalan menuju bagian dalam aula, dikelilingi oleh para penjaga.

“Yu Kecil, ayo pergi juga.”

Di belakangnya, Sekretaris Lin menepuk bahu Yu Qi dan menasihati, “Kamu adalah perwakilan kami di Front Persatuan Federasi Bumi. Kamu memiliki pengalaman diplomatik bertahun-tahun dan luar biasa dalam hal kemampuan kerja. Oleh karena itu, pemimpin meminta Kamu untuk berpartisipasi dalam pertemuan penting ini. ”

Saat berbicara, Sekretaris Lin diam-diam memperingatkan, “Ingat, lebih sedikit bicara dan amati lebih banyak pada pertemuan. Kamu harus mengingat setiap detail dalam pertemuan tersebut. Ini sangat penting bagi kami, mengerti? ”

“Iya!”

Melihat ini, Yu Qi secara alami tidak punya pilihan untuk melalaikan tugasnya. Dia mengangguk berulang kali. Setelah Sekretaris Lin melangkah maju, dia mengikuti di belakang dengan jarak yang sesuai.

Dapat berpartisipasi dalam konferensi besar seperti itu adalah kesempatan sekali seumur hidup yang tak terlupakan, kesempatan untuk terus mendaki dan bahkan memasuki inti kekuatan di Tiongkok!

Memikirkan hal ini, Yu Qi hanya merasakan lonjakan di hatinya. Dia dengan cepat menarik napas dalam-dalam dan mengikuti semua orang ke lift.

Namun, Yu Qi melihat tombol di lift tetapi menemukan bahwa tidak ada yang menekan tombol selama ini. Dia tidak bisa membantu tetapi melihat atasan langsungnya dengan bingung, tetapi pihak lain sepertinya tahu apa yang dia pikirkan dan hanya menggelengkan kepalanya dalam diam.

Sekitar sepuluh detik kemudian, pintu elevator ditutup dengan bunyi “ding”.

Yu Qi bertanya-tanya mengapa tidak ada yang menekan tombol apa pun di lift ketika dia tiba-tiba merasakan perasaan tidak berbobot. Dia tanpa sadar hanya melihat tampilan liftuntuk menemukan bahwa tampilan layar mengikuti angka negatif!

Dengan kata lain, elevator tidak menuju ke atas saat ini, tetapi menuju ke bawah!

Apa, setelah tinggal di sini begitu lama, dia bahkan tidak tahu bahwa ada fasilitas bawah tanah di markas Federasi Bumi?

Yu Qi sangat terkejut tetapi ketika lift tiba di lantainya, dia teringat sesuatu ketika dia melihat Sekretaris Lin melangkah keluar dari lift terlebih dahulu dan dengan terampil membimbing pria elegan itu ke depan.

Sekretaris Lin dulunya adalah juru bicara seperti Yu Qi. Setelah beberapa tahun berlatih dalam posisi ini di Front Bersatu, dia akhirnya menjadi yang teratas dan menjadi Sekretaris Lin saat ini…
