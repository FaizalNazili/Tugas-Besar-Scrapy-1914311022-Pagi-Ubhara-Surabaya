“Honestly, these treasures are already quite good. Items that Gold Immortals would collect are surely not ordinary items.” Chen Feng chuckled and kept the treasures.

He had swiftly sensed it earlier. There were a few Divine artifacts amongst the collected treasures. Though, more of them were naturally-occurring divine objects of chaos. That said, there were also some fragments of mid-grade Divine artifacts in the mix. But Chen Feng had no time to perform a detailed examination. It would be bad if the others come over to snatch the items from him.

And even though Tun Ri and the others were from the Celestial Longevity Plane, their faces – at that very moment – had become flushed. Most likely, they had gotten themselves quite the haul.

“As expected of a Graveyard of Divine Dragons. Unexpectedly, we would gain so much this time.”

The half-step Gold Immortals ignored Chen Feng’s group, but that did not mean that the others would do the same. Due to how far away they were, the other cultivators only managed to obtain 20% of the treasures that were flying around. As it was unrealistic to be competing against the half-step Gold Immortals for the two treasures, they turned their attention towards Chen Feng’s group instead.

“Friend, bring out the items and share it. You fellows cannot stomach so many items.” One of them, a high-level Heavenly Immortal with great combat power, charged forward. Killing intent surged out from his whole body.

“Someone wants to die. Kill them,” Chen Feng said calmly. The Divine Sun Needle in his hand flew out to instantly kill off two enemy cultivators.

“I’ve long since lost my patience! Behold, my Sun Devourer Technique!” Tun Ri displayed his innate skill while Da Feng and the rest attacked.

A chaotic and bloody battle ensued. Chen Feng’s group was from the Celestial Longevity Plane and their combat power exceeded that of the cultivators from the Immortal Plane. Every clash would result in the deaths of a high number of enemies. However, it did not take long for even more enemy cultivators to charge forward. Due to that, Tun Ri and the others began having a hard time. 

“I wonder if I can link up to the Celestial Longevity Plane here?” Tun Ri began growing enraged. He wanted to link up to the Celestial Longevity Plane to receive its support and increase his strength.

“Friend, as long as you take out half the treasures, we can forget about this.” The one who spoke up was already infinitely close to reaching the half-step Gold Immortal stage. With sword light swirling around him, he looked like someone from the Immortal Sword Sect. 

“Forget about this? No way,” Chen Feng said and he seemingly linked up to something. Next, the black spatial passageway re-appeared and a thick power of darkness gushed out from it.

Is the Young Master’s friend going to take action? Doesn’t the other party want to snatch the dragon corpse and dragon spear? Tun Ri felt puzzled.

A muscular Divine Mighty Ape jumped out from the spatial passageway. After appearing, its hand swung out to grab a Heavenly Immortal. Next, it roared and ripped the guy in half. It was a most bloody sight to behold.

“A Divine Mighty Ape at the high-level Heavenly Immortal stage. More, it cultivates the power of darkness!” Da Feng exclaimed.

It was followed by a second Divine Mighty Ape, then a third, a fourth and a fifth. After a total of five gigantic Divine Mighty Apes appeared, a Demonic Dragon of Darkness – 10,000 zhang in length – charged out from the black passageway. The aura of power that it emanated brought Chen Feng and the others a secret feeling of shock.

Demonic Shadow Panthers, Divine Skyroar Wolves, Phantom Beasts, other extra-terrestrial creatures filled with the power of darkness and several human cultivators shrouded in black energy streams appeared as well.

After over a hundred of them appeared, the black passageway disappeared. Every one of the reinforcements from the Dark Plane was a high-level Heavenly Immortal and they proceeded to unleash carnage the moment they appeared.

Add Tun Ri and the others into the equation, the situation changed completely and the cultivators who were besieging Chen Feng’s group were beaten so badly that bloody lights kept flaring out from their side. In the end, they had to run away.

“There are so many cultivators from the Dark Plane?” Tian Wu said, feeling surprised. As they were all extra-terrestrial creatures, Tian Wu felt a slight connection with those extra-terrestrial creatures of darkness.

By then, one of the half-step Gold Immortals had managed to grab hold of the dragon spear. However, several others besieged him, making it incredibly hard to escape.

“Pu!”

The half-step Gold Immortal decided to go the extra mile. Clenching his teeth, he then sprayed out a mouthful of his essence, energy and soul power to temporarily suppress the rampaging dragon spear. Then, with a swing, one of his opponents was sent flying. Following that, he continued brandishing the dragon spear and his combat power soared. After several exchanges, one of the half-step Gold Immortals became grievously wounded. As for the one with the dragon spear, by stimulating the power of the mid-grade Divine artifact, he fought his way forward, sweeping his opponents away. He was already on the verge of breaking out from their encirclement. 

“Stop him! He has yet to fully gain control over the dragon spear!”

“Once he can fully control it, all of us will die!”

All of the half-step Gold Immortals there had split up into two groups. One group went after the dragon corpse while the other went after the dragon spear. Someone had already obtained the dragon spear while the dragon corpse remained on the ground. It contained great powers and various cultivators kept fighting over it. And despite its toughness and power, it had been left there for too long. In the face of the all-out attacks from the half-step Gold Immortals, cracks began spreading out across the dragon corpse and streams of dragon essence wafted out.

In the end, the dragon corpse blew up with a bang and everyone there rushed to snatch the flesh, blood and bones of the dragon corpse.

The situation grew even more chaotic and Chen Feng’s group rushed forward as well. Grouping up into one force, they wanted to get a share of the pie.

This was a dragon corpse at the Gold Immortal stage. Even one drop of blood was a great harvest.

Meanwhile, the black passageway re-appeared as the gigantic black hand finally found the opportunity it needed. With one slap, it sent a half-step Gold Immortal flying and grabbed hold of the dragon spear. Next, it began leaving, closing the passageway behind it. 

Boom! Boom! Boom!

Several attacks burst forward and the black passageway finally disappeared. Next, a youngster in black clothes – wielding the dragon spear – strode forward. There was an indifferent expression on his face and black-coloured demonic runes kept emerging from his body. Radiating a ferocious atmosphere of majesty, he stabbed forward with the dragon spear to pierce one of the cultivators there, blowing up his body into two.

“Comparing with others is truly a vexing matter. I had thought that my rate of cultivation is fast. Unexpectedly, this fellow has already reached that level.” Watching the black-clad youngster begin a bloodbath, Chen Feng lamented.

“Young Master, this person exudes the aura of the Dark Plane’s essence!” Da Feng said, surprise in his tone. 

“He is my friend, the owner of the Dark Scripture. His strength has risen so quickly. He must have gained control over the Dark Plane.” After Chen Feng said that, his figure dashed forward. The Longevity Wings unfurled from his back and his speed skyrocketed as he rushed to grab the dragon talon that was flying towards him.  

As for the dragon head, he did not bother thinking about it. There was no telling just how many cultivators were fighting to the death over it. 

That said, even the dragon talon was not that easy to obtain. Before Chen Feng could even approach the dragon talon, a fine, golden-coloured blade of light slashed towards him. 

Although the blade of light appeared simplistic and without any special characteristics, Chen Feng could feel that the attack had locked down on both his soul and fleshly body. Even before the attack made contact, Chen Feng’s soul felt as though it was already being ripped apart. This was an attack from a half-step Gold Immortal and the targeting power of the grand dao laws. 

Not even the Longevity Wings was able to evade the targeting power of this attack. Thus, the Divine Sun Needle flashed out to collide against the attack. It managed to unravel part of the power behind the attack, but the attack struck Chen Feng in the end.

Boom!

Chen Feng’s figure was sent flying, leaving a thick energy wave in the sky. But with a twist, he stabilized himself. His face was somewhat ugly to behold and his body had been wounded somewhat. Even so, it was much lighter compared to the damage he received from the dragon spear’s attack earlier. 

A crack had also appeared on the chest portion of his suit of armour, but it swiftly closed up. 

You think you can break through my armour with an attack at the half-step Gold Immortal stage? This is the fragment of a high-grade Divine artifact! Chen Feng beckoned and the Divine Sun Needle flew into his hand. On the surface of the needle were some bloodstains that had yet to evaporate.

Tun Ri and Tian Wu moved forward to hold back the half-step Gold Immortal while Da Feng made use of the opportunity to grab the dragon talon.

“Scram!”

The half-step Gold Immortal became enraged. He was an exalted character. And yet, a mere handful of Heavenly Immortals managed to make a fool of him. More, these Heavenly Immortals were quite strong, capable of snatching a dragon talon away from him. 

Puchi!

The half-step Gold Immortal, wanting to unleash his might, failed to notice the dragon spear descending from the sky. It pierced his body. Next, the Dark Kirin grasped and the dragon spear spun swiftly. In the face of that formidable power, the half-step Gold Immortal’s body was ripped to bits. 

In just a short time, the Dark Kirin had seemingly gained control over the dragon spear. After he slaughtered several half-step Gold Immortals, the others no longer dared to attack him. They had to acknowledge that he was now the owner of the dragon spear. Some then turned to snatch the scattered parts of the dragon corpse. 

“Let’s go.”

The Dark Kirin then stretched his hand and grasped again. Space closed up around them and Chen Feng’s group was gathered. Following that, they felt the stars shifting. By the time they landed, they were already who knows how many li away from the tomb.

“Ha ha ha, it’s been a while since our last meeting. Unexpectedly, you have already reached the half-step Gold Immortal stage.” Turning to the Dark Kirin, Chen Feng smiled.

This was the trump card that Chen Feng had prepared. Before entering the Graveyard of Divine Dragons, he had already made contact with the Dark Kirin. Only, to serve as a hidden force, the Dark Kirin had been staying hidden all this time. His stealth technique was so perfect that not even the other half-step Gold Immortals were able to sense him. 

“I have completely fused with the Dark Scripture and become the true master of the Dark Plane. I’ve been spending the recent years fighting in the Dark Plane. The fact that I could advance to this level shocked me as well. Still, you have also managed to advance at an incredible rate.” The Dark Kirin had always maintained a cool demeanour, but the surprise in his eyes revealed his emotions.

“Greetings, senior.” Tun Ri and the others moved forward to greet the Dark Kirin. The Dark Kirin could be considered as a divine creature amongst the extra-terrestrial creatures. When it came to the nobility of his genes, he was in no way inferior to the divine dragons. Thus, it was only natural for Tun Ri and the rest to feel a kinship with him. 

“No need to stand on ceremony. Just address me as Dark Night,” The Dark Kirin said, nodding.

“Senior Dark Night, are there any other treasure hoards here?” Tun Ri stepped forward to ask inquisitively. 

“I was about to talk about that. This graveyard is very big and I can sense several more divine dragon corpses at the Gold Immortal stage. However, Gold Immortals have already appeared. We are no longer suited for what will happen next,” the Dark Kirin said.  

Bang!

A clump of golden light blew up in the distance and the resulting atmosphere of power caused the entire Graveyard of Divine Dragons to shake. 

“A battle between Gold Immortals!”

“Let’s go, we’ll leave this place!” The Dark Kirin swiftly took action. Bringing Chen Feng and the others along, he rapidly charged towards the outside world. 

Boom! Boom! Boom!

Golden light illuminated the whole place and the Graveyard of Divine Dragons that had remained indestructible all this time began showing cracks. Some unlucky Heavenly Immortals were swept into the cracks, never to emerge again. 

And despite the protection of the Dark Kirin’s power, Chen Feng’s group could still feel their scalps going numb. These were genuine Gold Immortals. Just a casual release of their auras would be enough to kill them off. 

“Chen Feng, save us!” As they were fleeing, Chen Feng heard someone call out his name. It sounded very urgent. By utilizing his eye technique, Chen Feng then found Sword Premier Demolisher and the others. They were quite unlucky. In the process of fleeing, they found themselves entrapped by the spatial cracks. Additionally, there were also chaotic streams of golden energy swirling in their surroundings.

Pertempuran kacau dan berdarah pun terjadi. Kelompok Chen Feng berasal dari Pesawat Panjang Umur Surga dan kekuatan tempur mereka melebihi para pembudidaya dari Pesawat Abadi. Setiap bentrokan akan mengakibatkan kematian sejumlah besar musuh. Namun, tidak butuh waktu lama bagi lebih banyak pembudidaya musuh untuk menyerang ke depan. Apakah teman Tuan Muda akan mengambil tindakan? Bukankah pihak lain ingin merebut mayat naga dan tombak naga? Tun Ri merasa bingung.

Kera Perkasa Ilahi yang berotot melompat keluar dari lorong spasial. Setelah muncul, tangannya terayun untuk meraih Dewa Surgawi. Selanjutnya, itu meraung dan merobek pria itu menjadi dua. Itu adalah pemandangan yang paling berdarah untuk dilihat.

Itu diikuti oleh Kera Perkasa Ilahi kedua, lalu yang ketiga, keempat dan kelima. Setelah total lima Kera Perkasa Ilahi raksasa muncul, Naga Kegelapan Iblis – panjangnya 10.000 zhang – menyerbu keluar dari lorong hitam. Aura kekuatan yang dipancarkannya membawa Chen Feng dan yang lainnya perasaan kaget rahasia.

Demonic Shadow Panthers, Divine Skyroar Wolves, Phantom Beasts, makhluk ekstra-terestrial lainnya yang diisi dengan kekuatan kegelapan dan beberapa pembudidaya manusia yang diselimuti aliran energi hitam juga muncul.

Setelah lebih dari seratus dari mereka muncul, lorong hitam menghilang. Setiap bala bantuan dari Dark Plane adalah Immortal Surgawi tingkat tinggi dan mereka mulai melepaskan pembantaian saat mereka muncul.

Pada saat itu, salah satu Dewa Emas setengah langkah berhasil meraih tombak naga. Namun, beberapa orang lainnya mengepungnya, membuatnya sangat sulit untuk melarikan diri.

Pu!

Gold Immortal setengah langkah memutuskan untuk bekerja ekstra. Mengepalkan giginya, dia kemudian menyemprotkan seteguk esensi, energi, dan kekuatan jiwanya untuk sementara menekan tombak naga yang mengamuk. Kemudian, dengan satu ayunan, salah satu lawannya dikirim terbang. Setelah itu, dia terus mengacungkan tombak naga dan kekuatan tempurnya melonjak. Setelah beberapa pertukaran, salah satu Dewa Emas setengah langkah menjadi sangat terluka. Adapun seseorang dengan tombak naga, dengan menstimulasi kekuatan artefak Divine kelas menengah, dia berjuang maju, menyapu musuhnya. Dia sudah di ambang keluar dari pengepungan mereka.

“Hentikan dia! Dia belum sepenuhnya menguasai tombak naga! ”

“Begitu dia bisa mengendalikannya sepenuhnya, kita semua akan mati!”

Semua Dewa Emas setengah langkah di sana telah dibagi menjadi dua kelompok. Satu kelompok mengejar mayat naga sementara yang lain mengejar tombak naga. Seseorang telah mendapatkan tombak naga sementara mayat naga tetap di tanah. Itu berisi kekuatan besar dan berbagai pembudidaya terus memperebutkannya. Dan terlepas dari ketangguhan dan kekuatannya, itu telah ditinggalkan di sana terlalu lama. Dalam menghadapi serangan habis-habisan dari Dewa Emas setengah langkah, retakan mulai menyebar ke seluruh tubuh naga dan aliran esensi naga berhembus keluar.

Pada akhirnya, mayat naga itu meledak dengan keras dan semua orang di sana bergegas untuk merebut daging, darah, dan tulang dari mayat naga itu.

Situasinya semakin kacau dan kelompok Chen Feng juga bergegas maju. Mengelompokkan menjadi satu kekuatan, mereka ingin mendapatkan bagian kue.

Ini adalah mayat naga di tahap Gold Immortal. Bahkan setetes darah pun merupakan panen yang besar.

Sementara itu, lorong hitam muncul kembali saat tangan hitam raksasa itu akhirnya menemukan kesempatan yang dibutuhkannya. Dengan satu tamparan, itu mengirim setengah langkah Emas Abadi terbang dan meraih tombak naga. Selanjutnya, ia mulai pergi, menutup lorong di belakangnya.

Ledakan! Ledakan! Ledakan!

Beberapa serangan meledak ke depan dan lorong hitam akhirnya menghilang. Selanjutnya, seorang anak muda berpakaian hitam – memegang tombak naga – melangkah ke depan. Ada ekspresi acuh tak acuh di wajahnya dan rune iblis berwarna hitam terus muncul dari tubuhnya. Memancarkan suasana keagungan yang ganas, dia menusuk ke depan dengan tombak naga untuk menembus salah satu pembudidaya di sana, meledakkan tubuhnya menjadi dua.

“Membandingkan dengan orang lain benar-benar masalah yang menjengkelkan. Aku berpikir bahwa tingkat kultivasi Aku cepat. Tanpa diduga, orang ini telah mencapai level itu. ” Menyaksikan anak muda berpakaian hitam memulai pertumpahan darah, Chen Feng meratap.

“Tuan Muda, orang ini memancarkan aura esensi Alam Gelap!” Kata Da Feng dengan nada terkejut.

Sayap Panjang Umur membentang dari punggungnya dan kecepatannya melonjak saat dia bergegas untuk meraih cakar naga yang terbang ke arahnya.

Adapun kepala naga, dia tidak repot-repot memikirkannya. Tidak ada yang tahu berapa banyak pembudidaya yang berjuang sampai mati untuk itu.

Konon, bahkan cakar naga pun tidak mudah didapat. Sebelum Chen Feng bahkan bisa mendekati cakar naga, pedang cahaya berwarna keemasan menebas ke arahnya.

Meskipun bilah cahaya tampak sederhana dan tanpa karakteristik khusus, Chen Feng dapat merasakan bahwa serangan itu telah mengunci jiwa dan tubuh dagingnya. Bahkan sebelum serangan itu terjadi, jiwa Chen Feng merasa seolah-olah sudah terkoyak. Ini adalah serangan dari Gold Immortal setengah langkah dan kekuatan penargetan dari hukum grand dao.

Bahkan Sayap Panjang Umur tidak mampu menghindari kekuatan penargetan dari serangan ini. Dengan demikian, Divine Sun Needle menyala untuk bertabrakan melawan serangan itu. Itu berhasil mengungkap sebagian dari kekuatan di balik serangan itu, tetapi serangan itu menyerang Chen Feng pada akhirnya.

Ledakan!

Sosok Chen Feng dikirim terbang, meninggalkan gelombang energi tebal di langit. Tetapi dengan suatu kejutan, dia menstabilkan dirinya sendiri. Wajahnya agak jelek untuk dilihat dan tubuhnya agak terluka. Meski begitu, jauh lebih ringan dibandingkan tkerusakan yang dia terima dari serangan tombak naga tadi.

Sebuah retakan juga muncul di bagian dada dari baju zirahnya, tapi dengan cepat menutup.

Kamu pikir kamu bisa menerobos armorku dengan serangan di tahap Gold Immortal setengah langkah? Ini adalah bagian dari artefak Divine bermutu tinggi! Chen Feng memberi isyarat dan Divine Sun Needle terbang ke tangannya. Di permukaan jarum ada beberapa noda darah yang belum menguap.

Tun Ri dan Tian Wu bergerak maju untuk menahan setengah langkah Emas Abadi sementara Da Feng memanfaatkan kesempatan untuk meraih cakar naga.

“Enyahlah!”

Gold Immortal setengah langkah menjadi marah. Dia adalah karakter yang diagungkan. Namun, hanya segelintir Dewa Surgawi yang berhasil membodohi dia. Terlebih lagi, Dewa Surgawi ini cukup kuat, mampu merebut cakar naga darinya.

Puchi!

Dewa Emas setengah langkah, ingin melepaskan kekuatannya, gagal memperhatikan tombak naga yang turun dari langit. Itu menembus tubuhnya. Selanjutnya, Dark Kirin menggenggam dan tombak naga itu berputar dengan cepat. Di hadapan kekuatan yang luar biasa itu, tubuh Gold Immortal setengah langkah itu robek berkeping-keping.

Hanya dalam waktu singkat, Dark Kirin tampaknya telah menguasai tombak naga. Setelah dia membantai beberapa Dewa Emas setengah langkah, yang lain tidak lagi berani menyerangnya. Mereka harus mengakui bahwa dia sekarang adalah pemilik tombak naga. Beberapa kemudian berbalik untuk merebut bagian-bagian tubuh naga yang berserakan.

“Ayo pergi.”

The Dark Kirin lalu mengulurkan tangannya dan menggenggam lagi. Ruang tertutup di sekitar mereka dan kelompok Chen Feng berkumpul. Setelah itu, mereka merasakan bintang-bintang bergeser. Pada saat mereka mendarat, mereka sudah tahu berapa banyak li dari kubur itu.

“Ha ha ha, sudah lama sejak pertemuan terakhir kita. Tanpa diduga, Kamu telah mencapai tahap Emas Abadi setengah langkah. ” Beralih ke Dark Kirin, Chen Feng tersenyum.

Ini adalah kartu truf yang telah disiapkan Chen Feng. Sebelum memasuki Makam Naga Ilahi, dia telah melakukan kontak dengan Dark Kirin. Hanya, untuk menjadi kekuatan tersembunyi, Dark Kirin telah bersembunyi selama ini. Teknik silumannya begitu sempurna sehingga Dewa Emas setengah langkah lainnya pun tidak bisa merasakannya.

“Aku telah benar-benar menyatu dengan Dark Scripture dan menjadi master sejati dari Dark Plane. Aku telah menghabiskan beberapa tahun terakhir untuk bertarung di Dark Plane. Fakta bahwa Aku bisa naik ke level ini juga mengejutkan Aku. Tetap saja, Kamu juga berhasil maju dengan kecepatan yang luar biasa. ” The Dark Kirin selalu mempertahankan sikap dingin, tapi keterkejutan di matanya mengungkapkan emosinya.

Salam, senior. Tun Ri dan yang lainnya bergerak maju untuk menyambut Dark Kirin. The Dark Kirin bisa dianggap sebagai makhluk ilahi di antara makhluk ekstra-terestrial. Ketika sampai pada kemuliaan gennya, dia sama sekali tidak kalah dengan naga dewa. Karena itu, wajar saja bagi Tun Ri dan yang lainnya merasakan kekeluargaan dengannya.

“Tidak perlu berdiri di atas upacara. Panggil saja aku sebagai Dark Night, ”kata The Dark Kirin sambil mengangguk.

“Senior Dark Night, apakah ada harta karun lain di sini?” Tun Ri melangkah maju untuk bertanya dengan rasa ingin tahu.

“Aku akan membicarakan tentang itu. Kuburan ini sangat besar dan Aku bisa merasakan beberapa mayat naga ilahi di tahap Abadi Emas. Namun, Dewa Emas telah muncul. Kami tidak lagi cocok untuk apa yang akan terjadi selanjutnya, ”kata Dark Kirin.

Bang!

Gumpalan cahaya keemasan meledak di kejauhan dan atmosfer kekuatan yang dihasilkan menyebabkan seluruh Makam Naga Ilahi bergetar.

“Pertarungan antara Dewa Emas!”

Ayo pergi, kita akan tinggalkan tempat ini! The Dark Kirin dengan cepat mengambil tindakan. Membawa Chen Feng dan yang lainnya, dia dengan cepat menyerang ke dunia luar.

Ledakan! Ledakan! Ledakan!

Cahaya keemasan menerangi seluruh tempat dan Makam Naga Ilahi yang selama ini tetap tidak bisa dihancurkan mulai menunjukkan retakan. Beberapa Dewa Surgawi yang tidak beruntung tersapu ke dalam celah, tidak pernah muncul lagi.

Dan terlepas dari perlindungan kekuatan Dark Kirin, kelompok Chen Feng masih bisa merasakan kulit kepala mereka mati rasa. Ini adalah Dewa Emas asli. Hanya dengan melepaskan aura mereka saja sudah cukup untuk membunuh mereka.”Chen Feng, selamatkan kami!” Saat mereka melarikan diri, Chen Feng mendengar seseorang memanggil namanya. Kedengarannya sangat mendesak. Dengan memanfaatkan teknik matanya, Chen Feng kemudian menemukan Sword Premier Demolisher dan yang lainnya. Mereka sangat tidak beruntung. Dalam proses pelarian, mereka menemukan diri mereka terperangkap oleh celah spasial. Selain itu, ada juga aliran energi emas yang kacau di sekitar mereka.
