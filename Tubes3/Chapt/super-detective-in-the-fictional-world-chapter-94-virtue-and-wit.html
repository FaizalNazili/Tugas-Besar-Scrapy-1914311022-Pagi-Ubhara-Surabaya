Chapter 94: Virtue and Wit

Translator: Henyee Translations  Editor: Henyee Translations

Luke could only grit his teeth.

He also cursed Tony Stark for his misfortune, even though he knew that Tony Stark wasn’t really responsible for his situation.

He knew a thing or two about Tony Stark.

From the movies he had watched in his last life, he knew that Tony Stark had a unique personality, and that he could be a great hero, but perhaps not a great friend.

It was completely understandable that Obadiah, his bald uncle, would try to kill him.

Tony Stark called Obadiah uncle. Their bond couldn’t be fake. Yet, the playboy couldn’t stop himself from squandering his wealth.

It was in his nature, and was hard to change.

Thus, Luke never intended to make friends with Tony Stark, because it was too difficult.

It wasn’t hard to imagine how haughty Stark was if he could shut the door on someone who had developed the ultimate virus1.

If Luke were to approach him, he would treat Luke like a servant and boss him around.

Luke spaced out, random thoughts running through his head.

To be honest, this security mission was absolutely pointless.

Tony Stark had a perfectly good security team to protect him. He didn’t need the police at all.

His bald uncle hadn’t decided to eliminate Tony Stark yet, so he should be safe for now.

This was only a gesture by the police department to win Tony Stark’s favor.

The start of the opening ceremony came and went. Everybody in the hall started whispering among themselves.

Luke didn’t even need to guess the reason.

It was Tony Stark’s natural propensity to be late, leave without saying goodbye, or stand other people up.

He had caused too much trouble before with his behavior.

From friend to foe, civilian to celebrity, he could stand anyone up.

Seeing that Selina was craning her head trying to catch the whispers, Luke said in a low voice, “There’s no need to think. One of Tony Stark’s greatest virtues is tardiness. I wouldn’t be surprised if he doesn’t show up at all today.”

Selina clicked her tongue. “But there are so many reporters and bosses here. Would he dare do that?”

Luke said, “Hm, I have no doubt that he would.”

It wasn’t until thirty minutes later that Tony Stark finally appeared, black circles around his eyes.

Luke figured that he probably had had too much fun last night with some girls who had sent themselves to his doorstep.

The opening ceremony finally began. Tony Stark took out a piece of paper and spoke dryly, before getting off the stage.

Luke even saw him fall asleep two minutes after he sat down.

There were other speeches in turn. Two hours later, the opening ceremony ended.

The real meeting wouldn’t begin until the afternoon. Corporation and research agency representatives would be locked in negotiations for three days.Read more chapter on vipnovel.com

That meant that Luke would have to suffer for three days.

After the opening ceremony, Luke and Selina had to help maintain order along with the other police officers.

Since they had more noteworthy ranks, Luke and Selina led the way for a bunch of corporate big-shots instead of joining the other officers who were holding the crowd back on the sides.

Tony Stark was in the front, next to Bill Yorlington, the CEO of Lockheed Martin Corporation.

Luke wasn’t paying much attention to them, but from his random glances, it was obvious that Tony Stark wasn’t interested in talking to his peer at all.

Bill, however, ignored it, and was just saying what he should say.

Well, Luke believed that Bill clearly wasn’t expecting to make friends with Tony. They were both pretending, except that Bill was a better actor.

Suddenly, a man squeezed through the wall of police officers and charged at Tony and Bill with a bottle.

The man approached from the right side, which Selina was responsible for.

Selina reacted swiftly. Confronted with the aggressive man, she calmly stepped to one side and grabbed the hand that was holding the bottle.

She then swept the guy’s feet out from under him, and pressed his hands to his back.

After that, she twisted the man’s wrist and forced him to drop the bottle, which rolled to the floor.

It was a nontransparent plastic bottle. Selina couldn’t see what was inside. She didn’t dare touch it, either.

However, both Luke and Selina noticed that the bottle was still sealed. They looked closer at the bottle, only to discover that it was milk.

Luke stepped forward and quickly handcuffed the guy, before he shoved him toward the other police officers.

The man was still shouting, “Stark, you war criminal, why are you still alive?”

Luke raised his eyebrow. This reason was all too understandable.

Anti-war groups often initiated protests, or more radical operations, against Stark’s super firearms enterprise.

Well, throwing milk at him was meant to humiliate him without actually hurting him. Similar weapons included raw eggs and potatoes.

Another police officer took away the bottle of, presumably, milk.

Before Luke and Selina could indicate to Tony Stark that he was safe now, he had already come over. His eyes glowed when he looked at Selina. “Wow, beautiful officer. Want to get a drink with me later?”

Luke, however, ignored him and said to Selina, “Stay alert.”

They returned to their posts, while the officers up ahead investigated the crowd. The team had stopped moving for the time being.

Tony was stunned. Had he been ignored?

He asked, “Gorgeous lady, can you give me your name? I think you and I could get to know each other better.”

Luke glanced back at him and said casually, “Mr. Stark, please do not interfere with our work.”

Tony raised his eyebrow. “Are you a gorgeous lady? Did you think I was talking to you?”

Luke suddenly smiled. “I saw the news about the cover girl of some magazine who was kicked out of your house naked in the middle of the night, and the one about the twin celebrities who said that you were impotent. So, I’m only hoping that the same tragedy won’t befall my partner, esteemed Mr. Stark.”

Selina was dazed. Luke spoke quickly and very clearly, and it had taken him no more than ten seconds.

Luke hanya bisa mengertakkan gigi.

Dia juga mengutuk Tony Stark atas kemalangannya, meskipun dia tahu bahwa Tony Stark tidak benar-benar bertanggung jawab atas situasinya.

Dia tahu satu atau dua hal tentang Tony Stark.

Dari film-film yang dia tonton di kehidupan terakhirnya, dia tahu bahwa Tony Stark memiliki kepribadian yang unik, dan bahwa dia bisa menjadi pahlawan yang hebat, tetapi mungkin bukan teman yang baik.

Dapat dimengerti sepenuhnya bahwa Obaja, pamannya yang botak, akan mencoba membunuhnya.

Tony Stark menelepon paman Obaja. Ikatan mereka tidak mungkin palsu. Namun, playboy itu tidak bisa menahan diri untuk menyia-nyiakan kekayaannya.

Itu sudah menjadi sifatnya, dan sulit untuk diubah.

Dengan demikian, Luke tidak pernah berniat berteman dengan Tony Stark, karena itu terlalu sulit.

Tidak sulit membayangkan betapa angkuhnya Stark jika dia bisa menutup pintu bagi seseorang yang telah mengembangkan virus utama1.

Jika Luke mendekatinya, dia akan memperlakukan Luke seperti pelayan dan memerintahnya.

Luke melamun, pikiran acak mengalir di kepalanya.

Sejujurnya, misi keamanan ini sama sekali tidak ada gunanya.

Tony Stark memiliki tim keamanan yang sangat baik untuk melindunginya. Dia sama sekali tidak membutuhkan polisi.

Pamannya yang botak belum memutuskan untuk melenyapkan Tony Stark, jadi dia seharusnya aman untuk saat ini.

Ini hanyalah isyarat dari departemen kepolisian untuk memenangkan hati Tony Stark.

Upacara pembukaan datang dan pergi. Semua orang di aula mulai berbisik di antara mereka sendiri.

Luke bahkan tidak perlu menebak alasannya.

Kecenderungan alami Tony Stark adalah terlambat, pergi tanpa pamit, atau membela orang lain.

Dia telah menyebabkan terlalu banyak masalah sebelumnya dengan perilakunya.

Dari teman ke musuh, sipil hingga selebriti, dia bisa membela siapa pun.

Melihat Selina menjulurkan kepalanya mencoba untuk menangkap bisikan, Luke berkata dengan suara rendah, “Tidak perlu berpikir. Salah satu kebajikan terbesar Tony Stark adalah keterlambatan. Aku tidak akan terkejut jika dia tidak muncul sama sekali hari ini. ”

Selina mendecakkan lidahnya. “Tapi ada banyak reporter dan bos di sini. Apakah dia berani melakukan itu? ”

Luke berkata, “Hm, Aku yakin dia akan melakukannya.”

Tidak sampai tiga puluh menit kemudian Tony Stark akhirnya muncul, lingkaran hitam di sekitar matanya.

Luke mengira bahwa dia mungkin bersenang-senang semalam dengan beberapa gadis yang telah mengirim diri ke depan pintunya.

Upacara pembukaan akhirnya dimulai. Tony Stark mengeluarkan selembar kertas dan berbicara dengan datar, sebelum turun dari panggung.

Luke bahkan melihatnya tertidur dua menit setelah dia duduk.

Ada pidato lain secara bergantian. Dua jam kemudian, upacara pembukaan berakhir.

Pertemuan yang sebenarnya tidak akan dimulai sampai sore hari. Perwakilan perusahaan dan badan penelitian akan terkunci dalam negosiasi selama tiga hari Baca bab lebih lanjut di vipnovel.com

Itu berarti Luke harus menderita selama tiga hari.

Usai acara pembukaan, Luke dan Selina harus membantu menjaga ketertiban bersama dengan petugas polisi lainnya.

Karena mereka memiliki pangkat yang lebih penting, Luke dan Selina memimpin jalan untuk sekelompok besar perusahaan alih-alih bergabung dengan petugas lain yang menahan kerumunan kembali di samping.

Tony Stark berada di depan, di samping Bill Yorlington, CEO Lockheed Martin Corporation.

Luke tidak terlalu memperhatikan mereka, tetapi dari pandangannya yang acak, terlihat jelas bahwa Tony Stark sama sekali tidak tertarik untuk berbicara dengan rekannya.

Bill, bagaimanapun, mengabaikannya, dan hanya mengatakan apa yang harus dia katakan.

Nah, Luke percaya bahwa Bill jelas-jelas tidak berharap bisa berteman dengan Tony. Mereka berdua berpura-pura, hanya saja Bill adalah aktor yang lebih baik.

Tiba-tiba, seorang pria menerobos dinding petugas polisi dan menuduh Tony dan Bill dengan botol.

Pria itu mendekat dari sisi kanan, yang menjadi tanggung jawab Selina.

Selina bereaksi dengan cepat. Dihadapkan dengan pria agresif itu, dia dengan tenang melangkah ke satu sisi dan meraih tangan yang memegang botol itu.

Dia kemudian menyapu kaki pria itu keluar dari bawahnya, dan menekankan tangannya ke punggung.

Setelah itu, dia memutar pergelangan tangan pria itu dan memaksanya untuk menjatuhkan botol yang berguling ke lantai.

Itu adalah botol plastik yang tidak transparan. Selina tidak bisa melihat apa yang ada di dalamnya. Dia juga tidak berani menyentuhnya.

Namun, Luke dan Selina menyadari bahwa botol itu masih tertutup rapat. Mereka melihat lebih dekat ke botol, hanya untuk menemukan bahwa itu wsebagai susu.

Luke melangkah maju dan dengan cepat memborgol pria itu, sebelum dia mendorongnya ke arah petugas polisi lainnya.

Pria itu masih berteriak, “Stark, kamu penjahat perang, kenapa kamu masih hidup?”

Luke mengangkat alisnya. Alasan ini terlalu bisa dimengerti.

Kelompok anti-perang sering memprakarsai protes, atau operasi yang lebih radikal, terhadap perusahaan senjata api super Stark.

Nah, melempar susu padanya dimaksudkan untuk mempermalukannya tanpa benar-benar menyakitinya. Senjata serupa termasuk telur mentah dan kentang.

Petugas polisi lainnya mengambil botol, mungkin, susu.

Sebelum Luke dan Selina bisa menunjukkan kepada Tony Stark bahwa dia sudah aman sekarang, dia sudah datang. Matanya bersinar saat dia melihat ke arah Selina. “Wah, petugas cantik. Mau minum bersamaku nanti? ”

Luke, bagaimanapun, mengabaikannya dan berkata kepada Selina, “Tetap waspada.”

Mereka kembali ke pos masing-masing, sementara petugas di depan menyelidiki kerumunan. Tim telah berhenti bergerak untuk sementara waktu.

Tony tercengang. Apakah dia telah diabaikan?

Dia bertanya, “Nona cantik, bisakah kamu memberikan namamu? Aku pikir Kamu dan Aku bisa lebih mengenal satu sama lain. ”

Luke balas menatapnya dan berkata dengan santai, “Mr. Stark, tolong jangan ganggu pekerjaan kami. ”

Tony mengangkat alisnya. “Apakah kamu seorang wanita cantik? Apakah Kamu pikir Aku sedang berbicara dengan Kamu? ”

Luke tiba-tiba tersenyum. “Aku melihat berita tentang gadis sampul sebuah majalah yang diusir dari rumahmu telanjang di tengah malam, dan tentang selebritis kembar yang mengatakan bahwa kamu impoten. Jadi, Aku hanya berharap tragedi yang sama tidak akan menimpa pasangan Aku, Tuan Stark yang terhormat. ”

Selina linglung. Luke berbicara dengan cepat dan sangat jelas, dan itu hanya membutuhkan waktu tidak lebih dari sepuluh detik.
