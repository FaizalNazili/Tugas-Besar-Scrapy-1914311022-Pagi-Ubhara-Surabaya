The curse and erosive Laws were a type of power that would extinguish all existences. Hence, as soon as Lin Li’s World Domain came into contact with the colorful smoke, it was immediately corroded without resistance. The Laws of the entire domain began to lax under the erosion of the poison, and when reflected in the world of the domain, it was like a huge disaster. There was a huge hole in the sky, and the ground was covered in poisonous swamps. In the blink of an eye, the entire world was full of destruction, and it seemed like it would be completely destroyed at any time.

When Lin Li cast his Domain World, he initially did not expect to rely on it to block out the Viper’s poison. However, the Viper’s poison was indeed a bit beyond Lin Li’s expectations, especially because it could spread along connections, making it truly impossible to guard against.

Seeing that his Domain World was about to collapse, Lin Li did not dare to delay any longer. After all, if his World Domain truly collapsed, it would have a huge impact on him, the host. He immediately waved his arm to toss out the debris of the stars, Holy Light. The pure power of Holy Light immediately formed a Light of Purification force field that fought against the evil aura of the entire space.

Although the power of Lin Li’s debris of the stars could not be maximized without the Stars of Fury, there was no longer a flaw in the attributes of power. However, Lin Li found it taxing when fighting against the colorful smoke. At the same time that the sinister and poisonous aura was being purified, the Light of Purification force field was continuously getting corroded too. The space was slowly being eaten away one bit at a time.

This is definitely not going to work! Lin Li knew very well in his heart that there would definitely be a moment where even the strongest defense would get broken. Only by turning the situation around and getting himself out of a passive position would he be able to defeat the prehistoric Viper.

However, the prehistoric Viper was not a clueless greenhorn. Besides, he could tell that the Light of Purification of Holy Light would not produce any good effects after coming into contact with Viper’s poison and the Power of Curses. Hence, the magic spells cast by the prehistoric Viper caught up with him. It was just like digging a hole in the land. Even if one filled it up with soil, it still wouldn’t be the same as the areas surrounding it.

It was the same for space. The magic spells of the prehistoric Viper had a huge corrosive property, so the sturdier spaces would naturally be different from the more fragile ones. Hence, the magic spells actually flowed over instead of chasing after him.

Damn it, just because I’m not showing my dominance, they think I’m a pushover! Although the magic spells flowed over, Lin Li was still extremely infuriated. The giant magic sword that Lin Li cast seemed to be extremely similar to the Light and Darkness Sword that he used in the past. Although it was also created with the fusion of light and darkness, in addition to the power of light and darkness, there was also a fusion of the other five basic World Laws. It was no longer considered the Light and Darkness Sword, and in fact, it was more reasonable to call it the World Sword which was as powerful as the power of the world. Even the World Domain of a Sanctuary powerhouse would be destroyed by it.

When the entire space was filled with the roars of the giant sword slashing through everything, the colorful smoke that shrouded the prehistoric Viper also began stirring as if it would be blown away like smoke in the wind at any time.

Lin Li was very confident in the power of his sword. Previously, he had faced many opponents in the Sanctuary-realm in the Barbarous Arena, all of whom were defeated by the World Sword. Although the prehistoric Viper was difficult to deal with, its power was near the Sanctuary-realm, and what made it difficult to defeat was its poison and curses.

However, what Lin Li did not expect was that the prehistoric Viper suddenly retracted the colorful smoke around it when faced with the falling World Sword. Immediately afterwards, a colorful snake shadow actually rushed forward to collide with the World Sword. The colorful shadow, which was formed by the colorful smoke, was like a clone of the prehistoric Viper. After being in contact with the World Sword, it even wrapped its body, which was hundreds of meters long, around the sword.

In the blink of an eye, the colorful snake shadow was wrapped entirely around the World Sword, making it seem as if the World Sword had been fitted with a colorful scabbard. Although the scabbard was used for protecting the sword, a large amount of poison immediately seeped into the World Sword, and the Laws that constituted the World Sword began cracking under the corrosion of the poison. When the World Sword was about to fall onto the head of the prehistoric Viper, the shadow of the colorful snake immediately dispersed, and reverted into the most poisonous colorful smoke while the World Sword vanished without a trace.

Lin Li was dumbfounded to see this outcome. The World Sword was a sword that was condensed from a world, and its power was probably the best of all Sanctuary-realm magic spells. He had thought of the possibility that the World Sword might be disintegrated by the prehistoric Viper, but the speed at which it happened was too exaggerated.

The prehistoric Viper did not intend to give Lin Li the time to recover from shock. After disintegrating the incredibly powerful World Sword, it seemed to be enraged by Lin Li’s attack. The horn on its head shone brilliantly, and the colorful smoke that filled the entire space immediately got riled up too, once again attacking Lin Li menacingly.

However, faced with the attacks of the prehistoric Viper, Lin Li looked a little smug. Not panicking at all, he pointed the Helios’ Scepter forward, and the colorful smoke that had begun stirring because of the prehistoric Viper once again calmed down. If one were to look carefully, they would realize that there was a vague dimness in the colorful smoke. As Lin Li triggered it, the inconspicuous dimness immediately erupted and swallowed the colorful smoke in the surroundings.

This time, it was the prehistoric Viper’s turn to panic. Although it was roaring with fury, the dimness was still expanding rapidly like ink dripping into water, staining the colorful smoke in the surroundings. In the blink of an eye, the colorful smoke turned into dark clouds that were as black as ink. It was extremely gloomy and no longer as radiant as before.

It’s my turn this time! Lin Li sneered and reached out to grab something in the air, after which the gloomy clouds began rolling and surging towards the prehistoric Viper in the middle. Soon, the body that was hundreds of meters long was covered as if it was a giant pupa.

Above the giant pupa, an extremely deep black sphere of light appeared in the void with two smaller spheres orbiting around it like a satellite. The black sphere was the debris of the stars Gloomy Dark, while the two smaller ones around it were the souls of the Double Death Dragons that had been sealed by Gloomy Dark.

Kali ini, giliran Viper prasejarah yang panik. Meski meraung karena amarah, keremangan itu masih meluas dengan cepat seperti tinta yang menetes ke air, menodai asap warna-warni di sekitarnya. Dalam sekejap mata, asap warna-warni berubah menjadi awan gelap yang sehitam tinta. Itu sangat suram dan tidak lagi bercahaya seperti sebelumnya.

Kali ini giliranku! Lin Li mencibir dan mengulurkan tangan untuk mengambil sesuatu di udara, setelah itu awan suram mulai bergulir dan melonjak menuju Viper prasejarah di tengah. Segera, tubuh yang panjangnya ratusan meter itu ditutupi seolah-olah itu adalah kepompong raksasa.

Di atas kepompong raksasa, bola cahaya hitam yang sangat pekat muncul di kehampaan dengan dua bola yang lebih kecil mengorbit di sekitarnya seperti satelit. Bola hitamDia adalah puing-puing bintang Gloomy Dark, sedangkan dua yang lebih kecil di sekitarnya adalah jiwa dari Naga Maut Ganda yang telah disegel oleh Gloomy Dark.
