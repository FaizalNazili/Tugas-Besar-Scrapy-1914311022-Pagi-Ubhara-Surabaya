Chapter 99 Cash Over Promotion

Of course, the playboy would find it difficult to get his wish.

But in any case, Tony Stark was a man of integrity, and the guys from the hunting club had none.

Thinking that, Luke’s eyes flashed coldly.

He pulled Selina out of the police station and whispered to her about the offer. The girl immediately shrieked, “Ah, Los Angeles? I’m going to Hollywood! Wait, where’s Hollywood in Los Angeles? Is it in our jurisdiction?”

Sweating hard, Luke replied, “The Westside Los Angeles Police Department covers Hollywood and West Hollywood. Beverly Hills and Sunset Boulevard are both there.”

Selina screamed again, “Ahhhh! I want to go! I have to go!”

Luke said, “…Do you have any money? You’ll starve if you don’t have money.”

Selina was frustrated. “Right, we don’t have any savings right now.”

Luke said, “That’s why I said to Chief Thomas that we would prefer cash over a promotion.”

Selina’s eyes bulged. “What? How’s that possible?”

Their transfer was weird and unconventional in the first place. Who would give them money?

Luke snorted. “If my guess is correct, a certain playboy who has too much money is behind this. The police departments in both Houston and Los Angeles will benefit a lot from our transfer. What’s wrong with asking for some money? I would rather not go if it means that we end up starving; it’s much cheaper to live in Houston, at least.”

Selina thought for a moment, but shook her head. “I wouldn’t count on us getting anything.”

Luke said, “Will you come with me if I do get us some money?”

Selina nodded quickly. “Of course. Actually, I want to go even if you don’t get any money.’

Luke said, “…You better call home and tell them about the transfer. I think it’ll happen soon.”

Things turned out exactly like he said.

Three days later, Luke and Selina set off on a Greyhound bus.

Luke’s only regret was that he couldn’t say goodbye to their “beloved” Officer Brock; when Luke left, the man had been suspended pending the FBI’s investigation.

Thomas brought them a piece of good news: the Los Angeles Police Department was willing to pay Luke and Selina a special relocation allowance.

Each of them got fifty thousand dollars, which was roughly their salary for an entire year.

Selina was confused at the reason for the money. She had never heard of a relocation allowance in her entire career before.

Officers in L.A. got a rent subsidy of two thousand dollars, not fifty thousand.

Luke merely chuckled. He knew that if Thomas had gotten a charity fund, the Los Angeles Police Department must’ve gotten something even bigger.

A hundred thousand dollars actually wasn’t a problem for the big shots in the Los Angeles Police Department, because they didn’t have to promote Luke and Selina now.

Even if Luke had asked for two hundred thousand, the Los Angeles Police Department would still have accepted it.

The Los Angeles Police Department was even more anxious than the Westside Houston Police Department was.

After the deal was made, the Los Angeles Police Department immediately urged them to come over, and even transferred them money for the plane tickets.

However… Selina said that the Greyhound bus would be more comfortable.

Of course, she only said that to Luke.

So, they took a Greyhound bus and headed west.

They saved on the money for plane tickets that way, since the Greyhound bus cost no more than one tenth of a flight. Naturally, the rest of the money went into their pockets.

Luke was fine with the bus.

Although they had just earned fifty thousand dollars each, it was never a bad idea to save money. They were told to report for duty in three days, and it would take two days at most to reach Los Angeles by bus.

Selina couldn’t take that many bags with her this time, so she simply packed up the apartment and left her luggage to her colleagues to send to her once she was settled.

They had gotten HPD a charity fund, so they didn’t feel guilty about the request at all.

What could Thomas say? He simply told the logistics division to handle the request.

Considering how much the Greyhound cost, it definitely wasn’t the most comfortable means of transport.

But Luke and Selina were both young and strong, and it wasn’t so bad for them.

They made it to Los Angeles in good time, and reported to the Los Angeles Police Department.

They had done it once just two months ago, so they were familiar with the procedure.

However, they had to take care of the paperwork themselves this time. They were assigned to the Major Crimes Division.

Luke was amused. They had been in the Major Crimes Division before, and were still in the same department after the transfer.

Selina was delighted to be assigned to the Westside Los Angeles Police Department; she would’ve accepted the transfer even without a relocation allowance.

Hearing that, Luke gloomily added, “You can’t live without money here. This is one of the most expensive districts in L.A. You better worry about your rent first.”

Selina was frustrated.

But soon, she was happy again. “That’s fine. Don’t we have a rent subsidy?”

Luke said, “Hehe. Do you think it’ll be enough for you to rent a shed?”

Selina glared at him. “Can you only bring up the annoying money stuff after I’ve enjoyed the wonders of this place?”

Luke said, “No, we have to find a place to stay first. We’ll use up the rent subsidy if we live in a hotel.”

They finally found a suitable apartment after three days of searching.

It was similar to their apartment in Houston.

It was a 50-square meter, two-bedroom apartment, and its only advantage was that it was already furnished with the basics.

The rent was twelve hundred dollars a month.

It would’ve been more than fifteen hundred if the apartment were slightly newer or had an elevator.

After they moved in, Selina cried, “This isn’t worth it! We’ll use up our relocation allowance on rent in a couple of years.”

Luke shrugged. “Don’t worry, I guarantee we’ll make a fortune.”

Selina wasn’t convinced. “We’re police officers. How do you plan to make a fortune?”

Pondering for a moment, Luke proposed, “How about we invent something?”

Selina laughed. “Tsk! You aren’t a kindergartner anymore. Please grow up.” She thought Luke was talking about inventing gadgets for children to play with.

Luke smiled but didn’t say anything.

He hadn’t felt the lack of money as much when he was in Houston. After all, Texas was one of the states with the lowest taxes, and California had some of the highest.

Their salaries were substantially higher after the transfer.

Bab 99 Promosi Tunai

Tentu saja, playboy akan kesulitan mendapatkan keinginannya.

Tapi bagaimanapun, Tony Stark adalah orang yang berintegritas, dan orang-orang dari klub berburu tidak punya.

Berpikir begitu, mata Luke berkedip dengan dingin.

Dia menarik Selina keluar dari kantor polisi dan berbisik padanya tentang tawaran itu. Gadis itu langsung berteriak, “Ah, Los Angeles? Aku akan ke Hollywood! Tunggu, di mana Hollywood di Los Angeles? Apakah itu di yurisdiksi kita? ”

Berkeringat keras, Luke menjawab, “Departemen Kepolisian Westside Los Angeles mencakup Hollywood dan Hollywood Barat. Beverly Hills dan Sunset Boulevard ada di sana. ”

Selina berteriak lagi, “Ahhhh! Aku ingin pergi! Aku harus pergi!”

Luke berkata, “… Apakah Kamu punya uang? Kamu akan kelaparan jika tidak punya uang. ”

Selina frustrasi. “Benar, kami tidak memiliki tabungan sekarang.”

Luke berkata, “Itu sebabnya Aku berkata kepada Chief Thomas bahwa kami lebih memilih uang tunai daripada promosi.”

Mata Selina melotot. “Apa? Bagaimana itu mungkin? ”

Transfer mereka aneh dan tidak konvensional sejak awal. Siapa yang akan memberi mereka uang?

Luke mendengus. “Jika tebakan Aku benar, ada seorang playboy yang memiliki terlalu banyak uang di balik ini. Departemen kepolisian di Houston dan Los Angeles akan mendapat banyak keuntungan dari pemindahan kami. Ada apa dengan meminta uang? Aku lebih suka tidak pergi jika itu berarti kita akhirnya kelaparan; jauh lebih murah untuk tinggal di Houston, setidaknya. ”

Selina berpikir sejenak, tapi menggelengkan kepalanya. “Aku tidak akan mengandalkan kita untuk mendapatkan apa pun.”

Luke berkata, “Maukah Kamu ikut dengan Aku jika Aku benar-benar memberi kami uang?”

Selina mengangguk dengan cepat. “Tentu saja. Sebenarnya, Aku ingin pergi meskipun Kamu tidak mendapatkan uang. ”

Luke berkata, “… Sebaiknya Kamu menelepon ke rumah dan memberi tahu mereka tentang transfer tersebut. Aku pikir itu akan segera terjadi. ”

Semuanya ternyata persis seperti yang dia katakan.

Tiga hari kemudian, Luke dan Selina berangkat dengan bus Greyhound.

Satu-satunya penyesalan Luke adalah dia tidak bisa mengucapkan selamat tinggal kepada Petugas Brock yang “dicintai”; ketika Luke pergi, pria itu telah ditangguhkan menunggu penyelidikan FBI.

Thomas membawakan mereka kabar baik: Departemen Kepolisian Los Angeles bersedia membayar tunjangan relokasi khusus kepada Luke dan Selina.

Masing-masing mendapat lima puluh ribu dolar, yang kira-kira merupakan gaji mereka untuk satu tahun penuh.

Selina bingung dengan alasan uang itu. Dia belum pernah mendengar tentang tunjangan relokasi sepanjang karirnya sebelumnya.

Petugas di L.A. mendapat subsidi sewa dua ribu dolar, bukan lima puluh ribu.

Luke hanya terkekeh. Dia tahu bahwa jika Thomas mendapatkan dana amal, Departemen Kepolisian Los Angeles pasti mendapatkan sesuatu yang lebih besar.

Seratus ribu dolar sebenarnya bukan masalah bagi orang-orang hebat di Departemen Kepolisian Los Angeles, karena mereka tidak perlu mempromosikan Luke dan Selina sekarang.

Bahkan jika Luke meminta dua ratus ribu, Departemen Kepolisian Los Angeles akan tetap menerimanya.

Departemen Kepolisian Los Angeles bahkan lebih cemas daripada Departemen Kepolisian Westside Houston.

Setelah kesepakatan dibuat, Departemen Kepolisian Los Angeles langsung mendesak mereka untuk datang, bahkan mentransfer uang untuk tiket pesawat.

Namun… Selina mengatakan bahwa bus Greyhound akan lebih nyaman.

Tentu saja, dia hanya mengatakan itu pada Luke.

Jadi, mereka naik bus Greyhound dan menuju ke barat.

Mereka menghemat uang untuk tiket pesawat dengan cara itu, karena harga bus Greyhound tidak lebih dari sepersepuluh penerbangan. Secara alami, sisa uang masuk ke kantong mereka.

Luke baik-baik saja dengan bus.

Meskipun mereka masing-masing baru saja mendapatkan lima puluh ribu dolar, menabung bukanlah ide yang buruk. Mereka disuruh melapor untuk bertugas dalam tiga hari, dan itu akan memakan waktu paling lama dua hari untuk mencapai Los A.ngeles dengan bus.

Selina tidak bisa membawa banyak tas bersamanya kali ini, jadi dia hanya mengemasi apartemen dan menyerahkan barang bawaannya kepada rekan-rekannya untuk dikirimkan kepadanya begitu dia sudah beres.

Mereka telah mendapatkan dana amal untuk HPD, jadi mereka sama sekali tidak merasa bersalah atas permintaan tersebut.

Apa yang bisa Thomas katakan? Dia hanya menyuruh divisi logistik untuk menangani permintaan tersebut.

Mempertimbangkan berapa harga Greyhound, itu jelas bukan alat transportasi yang paling nyaman.

Tapi Luke dan Selina sama-sama muda dan kuat, dan itu tidak terlalu buruk bagi mereka.

Mereka berhasil sampai di Los Angeles dengan waktu yang tepat, dan melapor ke Departemen Kepolisian Los Angeles.

Mereka melakukannya sekali hanya dua bulan yang lalu, jadi mereka terbiasa dengan prosedurnya.

Namun, kali ini mereka harus mengurus sendiri dokumennya. Mereka ditugaskan ke Divisi Kejahatan Besar.

Luke merasa geli. Mereka pernah berada di Divisi Kejahatan Besar sebelumnya, dan masih berada di departemen yang sama setelah pemindahan.

Selina sangat senang ditugaskan di Departemen Kepolisian Westside Los Angeles; dia akan menerima transfer bahkan tanpa tunjangan relokasi.

Mendengar itu, Luke dengan muram menambahkan, “Kamu tidak bisa hidup tanpa uang di sini. Ini adalah salah satu distrik termahal di L.A. Lebih baik Kamu mengkhawatirkan uang sewa dulu. ”

Selina frustrasi.

Tapi segera, dia bahagia lagi. “Tidak apa-apa. Bukankah kita punya subsidi sewa? ”

Luke berkata, “Hehe. Apakah menurut Kamu itu cukup bagi Kamu untuk menyewa gudang? ”

Selina memelototinya. “Bisakah kamu mengungkit soal uang yang mengganggu setelah aku menikmati keajaiban tempat ini?”

Luke berkata, “Tidak, kita harus mencari tempat tinggal dulu. Kami akan menggunakan subsidi sewa jika kami tinggal di hotel. ”

Mereka akhirnya menemukan apartemen yang cocok setelah tiga hari mencari.

Itu mirip dengan apartemen mereka di Houston.

Itu adalah apartemen dua kamar tidur seluas 50 meter persegi, dan satu-satunya keuntungan adalah bahwa itu sudah dilengkapi dengan perlengkapan dasar.

Biaya sewanya dua belas ratus dolar sebulan.

Itu akan menjadi lebih dari seribu lima ratus jika apartemen itu sedikit lebih baru atau memiliki lift.

Setelah mereka pindah, Selina berteriak, “Ini tidak layak! Kami akan menggunakan tunjangan relokasi kami untuk sewa dalam beberapa tahun. ”

Luke mengangkat bahu. Jangan khawatir, Aku jamin kita akan menghasilkan banyak uang.

Selina tidak yakin. Kami adalah petugas polisi. Bagaimana Kamu berencana untuk menghasilkan banyak uang? ”

Merenungkan sejenak, Luke mengusulkan, “Bagaimana kalau kita menemukan sesuatu?”

Selina tertawa. “Cih! Kamu bukan anak taman kanak-kanak lagi. Harap tumbuh dewasa. ” Dia pikir Luke sedang berbicara tentang menciptakan gadget untuk dimainkan anak-anak.

Luke tersenyum tetapi tidak mengatakan apa-apa.

Dia tidak pernah merasakan kekurangan uang sebanyak dia berada di Houston. Bagaimanapun, Texas adalah salah satu negara bagian dengan pajak terendah, dan California memiliki beberapa yang tertinggi.

Gaji mereka jauh lebih tinggi setelah transfer.
