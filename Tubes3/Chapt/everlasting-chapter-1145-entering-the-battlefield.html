Although the Longevity Tower was only heading out to lure the enemy away, Chen Feng had a feeling that the Longevity Tower would not return anytime soon.

Following the Longevity Tower’s departure, Chen Feng had first felt somewhat uncomfortable and empty. However, his heart quickly relaxed.

Only then did Chen Feng realize how much he had been relying on the Longevity Tower all this time. Thanks to the changes to his state of mind, Chen Feng then entered a wondrous state.

An epiphany from the depths of the soul! Tie Zhen and the others exchanged glances before silently backing away. Some of them even revealed a slight look of envy. An epiphany of this level was incredibly rare, comparable even to over 10,000 years of bitter effort.

As expected of the most noble bloodline! Tie Zhen secretly mused.

Even after the warship had arrived, Chen Feng was still immersed in the transformation of his state of mind. As for Tie Zhen and the others, they kept standing guard over Chen Feng, not allowing anyone to approach him so as to not disrupt him.

Chen Feng was experiencing a very special change. His soul power was not growing stronger, but his state of mind was clearly improving. This was a qualitative improvement, akin to a large dog transforming into a young tiger. And although its strength was still very small, its room for growth had expanded.

His divine sense spread across the warship. And although the amount of soul power he was using was similar to the past, the scope had increased by 10%.

Over 100,000 cultivators were in the midst of a bloody battle and the sky was dyed red. Mountains cracked and the lands roiled. Various chaotic powers kept rampaging about and highly-compressed pillars of energy kept flying around. Those were the energy cannons, which roared loudly.

This was a battlefield that would only appear in a high-level world like the Immortal Plane. Any participants in this battle would be considered as a pinnacle expert in the lesser worlds. 

The 10,000 plus cultivators from the Six Daos Sword Faction had already joined the war. Even so, the situation remained unfavourable to them. The Six Daos Sword Faction had to face the combined attacks of two other forces. They could not neutralize the pressure nor avoid the casualties of this war.

As for Sword Premier Demolisher and the other high-level Heavenly Immortals, they were fighting within an independent stretch of space. Besieged, they were in a disadvantageous spot. Even in the grand scheme of the war, the tens of thousands of cultivators from the Six Daos Sword Faction were constantly forced to give ground. Should this be allowed to continued, it would end with the collapse of the Six Daos Sword Faction’s army.

“There are 18 spiritual fountains here. These fountains are the objectives of the two warring parties,” said one of Chen8’s bodyguard named Jin Feng, a mid-level Heavenly Immortal.  

“What grade of spiritual fountains?” Chen Feng grew interested.

“These are spiritual fountains containing the grand dao of Heaven. It can benefit cultivators up to the high-level Heavenly Immortal stage. Even True Immortals and Ascendant Immortals can absorb the spiritual energy there to cultivate.” Jin Feng had already inquired the details of the spiritual fountains.

“These are good items,” Chen Feng said, flying out from the warship to join the battlefield. Tie Zhen’s team of six followed him closely.

The moment he approached the battlefield, hundreds of attacks shot towards Chen Feng like a hail of arrows. However, even the strongest amongst the attacks were from a half-step Heavenly Immortal. This was due to Chen Feng’s cultivation base at the True Immortal stage. Thus, Heavenly Immortals were not dispatched against him. Only some Heavenly Immortals with formidable cultivation bases were able to sense something amiss.

Before the attacks could approach Chen Feng, swift and forceful sword beams shredded the attacks to bits. Next, the sword beams swirled around before expanding into his surroundings. By the time the sword beams dissipated, over 100 enemy cultivators had died to the sword beams.

“What? They have an expert!”

“Fellow cultivator! As a Heavenly Immortal, killing True Immortals is a violation of the laws and regulations of the Immortal Plane.” Seeing what happened, a Heavenly Immortal there grew furious. With a wave of his hand, he formed a great starlight hand, which then pressed down on Chen Feng.

Shua!

A sword beam flashed out and the great starlight hand was cut down. At the same time, the Heavenly Immortal’s entire arm shattered apart.

“You’re a high-level Heavenly Immortal. Attacking a True Immortal is a violation of the Immortal Plane’s rules, no?” Tie Zhen said.

“How can he be a True Immortal?” Envoy Heavenly Sword from the Immortal Sword Sect spoke up, his voice drifting out before transforming into a flying sword, which shot towards Chen Feng.

“Courting death!” Tie Zhen’s battle lance swung out to shatter the flying sword. Next, the battle lance stabbed forward and a large hole appeared on Envoy Heavenly Sword’s body. 

“Tie Zhen, go kill him,” Chen Feng said.

“But…” Tie Zhen felt somewhat concerned.

“Don’t worry. With the five of them following me, there will not be any dangers,” Chen Feng said, the sword in his hand slashing forward to form a Storm of Sword Beams, which swept far into the distance.

Due to Chen Feng’s insistence, Tie Zhen nodded. His entire being seemingly became upright. Wielding his battle lance, he then strode forward. With one step, he broke space. With the second step, he entered the battle dimension of the Heavenly Immortals. 

“Die!”

Every single move from Tie Zhen was clean and quick. The battle lance in his hand stabbed out with lightning-like speed and Envoy Heavenly Sword was pierced by the lance. Envoy Heavenly Sword had wanted to evade but failed. In the end, he was killed off. Only after he had died did the few cultivators there howl out and their attacks surged forward.

A lance silhouette abruptly burst into two before bursting into four. In the end, it became countless and the cultivators attacking Tie Zhen were all sent tumbling backwards.

“How is this possible?!”

The cultivators from the Starsource Order and Immortal Sword Sect exchanged glances. They were all high-level Heavenly Immortals. How could there be such a big difference between them? Then, they thought back to the True Immortal from earlier. Perhaps, he was really a True Immortal.

“Ha ha ha ha ha! Chen Qing is indeed a True Immortal of our Six Daos Sword Faction! Our Six Daos Sword Faction has yet to stoop so low as to bully those at a lower level!” Sword Premier Demolisher laughed loudly.

“Sword Premier Demolisher, why must it come to this? As long as your side agrees to it, we will be able to negotiate this peacefully. Why must it escalate to the point where we cannot even negotiate?” Suddenly, Envoy Starbreak from the Starsource Order spoke up. At the same time, he and Envoy Crescent Moon cast a glance at Tie Zhen. They had determined that Tie Zhen was the one who teleported away the warship back then.

“You fellows are the ones who started this war. Although our Six Daos Sword Faction has fallen, we are still not something that sects like yours can bully. You want to exterminate our Six Daos Sword Faction? Are you aware of what kind of price you have to pay for that?” Sword Premier Demolisher laughed loudly. Even with Tie Zhen’s participation, their side was still in a disadvantageous position. However, Tie Zhen’s strength had given him slightly more confidence.

Only, since killing off Envoy Heavenly Sword, Tie Zhen had not taken the initiative to attack anymore. Turning to look at Chen Feng, he then saw a display of dominance from him. Killing intent surged to the sky as the sword in his hand swung and sword beams rampaged about. Jin Feng and the other four bodyguards followed Chen Feng while keeping a distance from him, not attacking at all. Some starter-level Heavenly Immortals were sent flying before they could even approach the five bodyguards. Due to that, everyone there realized just how strong the five of them were and they dared not approach them anymore.

Over 300 True Immortals had fallen to Chen Feng. Next, the sword beam he fired out cut through space and his figure flashed forward to enter yet another battlefield.

The number of cultivators in this battlefield also exceeded a thousand. All of them were Heavenly Immortals, one level higher compared to the battlefield of True Immortals that he was in earlier.

Entering the battlefield, Chen Feng was immediately targeted by the combined attacks from tens of Heavenly Immortals. Seeing that, Jin Feng and the others wanted to immediately rush forward.

“I’ll deal with this. You fellows stand aside.” Chen Feng pushed out with both hands and a shield appeared before him. It was none other than the Longevity Shield that had levelled up to the pseudo-Divine tier.

All the attacks fused as one before landing on the Longevity Shield. Instantly, Chen Feng felt as though a river of stars from the Nine Heavens was scouring him. The Longevity Shield was only able to endure for one breath’s worth of time. The Longevity runes on its surface burst apart in quick succession before the shield itself disintegrated. The remaining energy streams then scoured Chen Feng’s body and multi-coloured streams of light engulfed Chen Feng.

The faces of Jin Feng and the other four bodyguards sank and they wanted to rush forward only to be stopped by tens of enemy cultivators. Even Tie Zhen’s face sank, but two enemy cultivators stopped him.

Due to their feelings of concern, Tie Zhen and the others stopped holding back. They unleashed a tempestuous wave of attacks which quickly killed off tens of Heavenly Immortals.

“The Longevity Clan! These fellows are from the Longevity Clan!”

Someone exclaimed and the Heavenly Immortals from the Starsource Order and Immortal Sword Sect revealed looks of concern.

“Don’t worry. I am fine.” Chen Feng’s voice then made its way to the ears of his six bodyguards. After the streams of light dissipated away, Chen Feng could be seen there, utterly unscathed. The power of laws intertwined one another as a suit of combat armour covered in various runes wrapped around Chen Feng.

The Longevity Combat Armour! The eyes of Jin Feng and the others lit up.

A pity, it is only at the pseudo-Divine tier. That said, it contains the power of 64 Heavenly laws. It should already be comparable to a genuine Divine artifact. Chen Feng kicked with both legs to disappear from where he was standing and the attacks descending from the sky missed. Chen Feng then charged into a highly-packed battleground.

Earlier, Chen Feng’s fight against the True Immortals was practically a slaughter fest and even Chen Feng felt like a bully. Only now was he able to feel pressured. The combined attacks from the tens of Heavenly Immortals earlier managed to break apart the Longevity Shield and even force Chen Feng to retreat quite a bit.

Without the Longevity Tower to serve as insurance and backing, everything came down to Chen Feng alone. In the face of the pressure, Chen Feng kept killing his way forward. Finally, he was able to feel the satisfaction of battle.

Of the two high-level Heavenly Immortals who were besieging Tie Zhen, one was killed while the other was badly wounded. Tie Zhen, on the other hand, was not satisfied with this result. Only, seeing Chen Feng safe and sound, he grew relieved.

“Fellow cultivator, why would your Celestial Longevity Plane interfere in the war between our side and the Six Daos Sword Faction? Are you fellows perhaps interested in the spiritual fountains here? If that’s the case, just say the word and we will retreat.” There was a hint of awe in the way Envoy Starbreak looked at Tie Zhen.

Due to the war a million years ago, the Celestial Longevity Plane’s strength had fallen somewhat. Even so, its exalted position was not something that forces like the Starsource Order could contend against. 

Existences like the Celestial Planes were for the Immortal Court to worry about. The Starsource Order and Six Daos Sword Faction were incapable of withstanding even a finger from the other party.

Tie Zhen ignored them. With one step, he left this battlefield to enter the battlefield that Chen Feng was in. The battle lance in his hand swung out and the tens of Heavenly Immortals blocking his way were all killed.

No one dared to speak up and criticize Tie Zhen.

“Let’s just retreat.” Envoy Starbreak sighed.

Chen Feng, who was having his fill of fighting in the battlefield, suddenly felt the pressure on him lessening. Only then did he notice that the other party was retreating. Seeing that, the cultivators from the Six Daos Sword Faction’s side, who were already on the verge of losing, let out howls of surprised delight.

Looking around, Chen Feng immediately understood what happened. 

Looks like I cannot stay in the Six Daos Sword Faction anymore.

Meskipun Menara Umur Panjang hanya menuju untuk memancing musuh pergi, Chen Feng merasa bahwa Menara Umur Panjang tidak akan kembali dalam waktu dekat.

Setelah kepergian Menara Umur Panjang, Chen Feng pertama kali merasa agak tidak nyaman dan kosong. Namun, hatinya cepat rileks.

Baru saat itulah Chen Feng menyadari betapa dia telah mengandalkan Menara Umur Panjang selama ini. Berkat perubahan keadaan pikirannya, Chen Feng kemudian memasuki keadaan yang menakjubkan.

Sebuah pencerahan dari kedalaman jiwa! Tie Zhen dan yang lainnya bertukar pandang sebelum diam-diam mundur. Beberapa dari mereka bahkan menunjukkan sedikit rasa iri. Pencerahan tingkat ini sangat langka, bahkan sebanding dengan upaya pahit selama lebih dari 10.000 tahun.

Seperti yang diharapkan dari garis keturunan paling mulia! Tie Zhen diam-diam merenung.

Bahkan setelah kapal perang tiba, Chen Feng masih tenggelam dalam transformasi pikirannya. Adapun Tie Zhen dan yang lainnya, mereka terus menjaga Chen Feng, tidak membiarkan siapa pun mendekatinya agar tidak mengganggunya.

Chen Feng mengalami perubahan yang sangat istimewa. Kekuatan jiwanya tidak tumbuh lebih kuat, tetapi keadaan pikirannya jelas membaik. Ini adalah peningkatan kualitatif, mirip dengan anjing besar yang berubah menjadi harimau muda. Dan meskipun kekuatannya masih sangat kecil, ruang pertumbuhannya telah meluas.

Perasaan ilahi-Nya menyebar ke seluruh kapal perang. Dan meskipun jumlah kekuatan jiwa yang dia gunakan mirip dengan masa lalu, cakupannya telah meningkat 10%.

Lebih dari 100.000 pembudidaya berada di tengah-tengah pertempuran berdarah dan langit diwarnai merah. Pegunungan retak dan tanah bergolak. Berbagai kekuatan kacau terus mengamuk dan pilar energi yang sangat terkompresi terus terbang. Itu adalah meriam energi, yang meraung keras.

Ini adalah medan perang yang hanya akan muncul di dunia tingkat tinggi seperti Pesawat Abadi. Setiap peserta dalam pertempuran ini akan dianggap sebagai ahli puncak di dunia yang lebih rendah.

10.000 lebih pembudidaya dari Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao telah bergabung dalam perang. Meski begitu, situasinya tetap tidak menguntungkan bagi mereka. Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao harus menghadapi serangan gabungan dari dua kekuatan lainnya. Mereka tidak bisa menetralkan tekanan atau menghindari korban dari perang ini.

Adapun Sword Premier Demolisher dan Dewa Surgawi tingkat tinggi lainnya, mereka bertarung dalam bentangan ruang yang independen. Terkepung, mereka berada di tempat yang tidak menguntungkan. Bahkan dalam skema besar perang, puluhan ribu pembudidaya dari Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao terus-menerus dipaksa untuk memberikan tanah. Jika ini dibiarkan berlanjut, itu akan berakhir dengan runtuhnya pasukan Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao.

“Ada 18 air mancur spiritual di sini. Air mancur ini adalah tujuan dari dua pihak yang bertikai, “kata salah satu pengawal Chen8 bernama Jin Feng, Dewa Surgawi tingkat menengah.

“Air mancur spiritual kelas berapa?” Chen Feng menjadi tertarik.

“Ini adalah air mancur spiritual yang berisi grand dao Surga. Ini dapat memberi manfaat bagi para pembudidaya hingga tahap Surgawi Abadi tingkat tinggi. Bahkan Dewa Sejati dan Dewa Ascendant dapat menyerap energi spiritual di sana untuk berkultivasi. ” Jin Feng sudah menanyakan detail air mancur spiritual.

“Ini adalah barang bagus,” kata Chen Feng, terbang keluar dari kapal perang untuk bergabung dengan medan perang. Tim enam Tie Zhen mengikutinya dari dekat.

Saat dia mendekati medan perang, ratusan serangan ditembakkan ke arah Chen Feng seperti hujan panah. Namun, bahkan yang terkuat di antara serangan itu berasal dari Dewa Surgawi setengah langkah. Ini karena basis kultivasi Chen Feng di tahap True Immortal. Dengan demikian, Dewa Surgawi tidak dikirim untuk melawannya. Hanya beberapa Dewa Surgawi dengan basis kultivasi yang tangguh yang dapat merasakan sesuatu yang salah.

Sebelum serangan bisa mendekati Chen Feng, sinar pedang yang cepat dan kuat menghancurkan serangan itu menjadi berkeping-keping. Selanjutnya, sinar pedang berputar-putar sebelum meluas ke sekelilingnya. Pada saat balok pedang menghilang, lebih dari 100 pembudidaya musuh telah mati karena balok pedang.

“Apa? Mereka memiliki seorang ahli! ”

“Rekan kultivator! Sebagai Dewa Surgawi, membunuh Dewa Sejati adalah pelanggaran terhadap hukum dan peraturan dari Alam Abadi. ” Melihat apa yang terjadi, Dewa Surgawi di sana menjadi sangat marah. Dengan lambaian tangannya, dia membentuk tangan cahaya bintang yang besar, yang kemudian menekan Chen Feng.

Shua!

Sinar pedang bersinar dan tangan cahaya bintang yang besar itu telah ditebas. Pada saat yang sama, Dewa SurgawiSeluruh lengan Aku hancur berantakan.

“Kamu adalah Dewa Surgawi tingkat tinggi. Menyerang True Immortal adalah pelanggaran terhadap aturan Immortal Plane, bukan? ” Kata Tie Zhen.

“Bagaimana dia bisa menjadi Dewa Sejati?” Utusan Pedang Surgawi dari Sekte Pedang Abadi angkat bicara, suaranya melayang sebelum berubah menjadi pedang terbang, yang ditembakkan ke arah Chen Feng.

“Mengadili kematian!” Tombak tempur Tie Zhen terayun untuk menghancurkan pedang terbang itu. Selanjutnya, tombak perang itu menusuk ke depan dan sebuah lubang besar muncul di tubuh Utusan Pedang Surgawi.

“Tie Zhen, bunuh dia,” kata Chen Feng.

“Tapi …” Tie Zhen merasa agak khawatir.

“Jangan khawatir. Dengan lima dari mereka mengikutiku, tidak akan ada bahaya, ”kata Chen Feng, pedang di tangannya menebas ke depan untuk membentuk Badai Pedang, yang menyapu jauh ke kejauhan.

Karena desakan Chen Feng, Tie Zhen mengangguk. Seluruh tubuhnya tampaknya menjadi tegak. Sambil memegang tombak tempurnya, dia lalu melangkah maju. Dengan satu langkah, dia memecahkan ruang. Dengan langkah kedua, dia memasuki dimensi pertempuran Dewa Surgawi.

“Mati!”

Setiap gerakan dari Tie Zhen bersih dan cepat. Tombak pertempuran di tangannya ditusuk dengan kecepatan seperti kilat dan Pedang Surgawi Utusan ditusuk oleh tombak. Pedang Surgawi Utusan ingin menghindar tetapi gagal. Pada akhirnya, dia terbunuh. Hanya setelah dia meninggal, beberapa pembudidaya di sana melolong dan serangan mereka melonjak ke depan.

Siluet tombak tiba-tiba meledak menjadi dua sebelum meledak menjadi empat. Pada akhirnya, itu menjadi tak terhitung jumlahnya dan para pembudidaya yang menyerang Tie Zhen semuanya dikirim jatuh ke belakang.

“Bagaimana ini mungkin?!”

Para pembudidaya dari Starsource Order dan Immortal Sword Sect bertukar pandang. Mereka semua Dewa Surgawi tingkat tinggi. Bagaimana bisa ada perbedaan besar di antara mereka? Kemudian, mereka berpikir kembali ke True Immortal dari sebelumnya. Mungkin, dia benar-benar seorang Immortal Sejati.

“Ha ha ha ha ha! Chen Qing memang Dewa Sejati dari Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao kami! Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao kita masih belum membungkuk begitu rendah untuk menindas orang-orang di level yang lebih rendah! ” Sword Premier Demolisher tertawa keras.

“Sword Premier Demolisher, mengapa harus seperti ini? Selama pihak Kamu menyetujuinya, kami akan dapat menegosiasikan ini dengan damai. Mengapa harus meningkat ke titik di mana kita bahkan tidak bisa bernegosiasi? ” Tiba-tiba, Utusan Starbreak dari Starsource Order angkat bicara. Pada saat yang sama, dia dan Utusan Bulan Sabit melirik Tie Zhen. Mereka telah menentukan bahwa Tie Zhen adalah orang yang memindahkan kapal perang saat itu.

“Kalian adalah orang-orang yang memulai perang ini. Meskipun Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao kami telah jatuh, kami masih bukan sesuatu yang sekte seperti milik Kamu dapat menggertak. Kamu ingin memusnahkan Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao kami? Apakah Kamu tahu berapa harga yang harus Kamu bayar untuk itu? ” Sword Premier Demolisher tertawa keras. Bahkan dengan partisipasi Tie Zhen, pihak mereka masih dalam posisi yang tidak menguntungkan. Namun, kekuatan Tie Zhen telah memberinya sedikit kepercayaan diri.

Hanya, sejak membunuh Envoy Heavenly Sword, Tie Zhen tidak mengambil inisiatif untuk menyerang lagi. Beralih untuk melihat Chen Feng, dia kemudian melihat tampilan dominasi darinya. Niat membunuh melonjak ke langit saat pedang di tangannya terayun dan balok pedang mengamuk. Jin Feng dan empat pengawal lainnya mengikuti Chen Feng sambil menjaga jarak darinya, tidak menyerang sama sekali. Beberapa Dewa Surgawi tingkat pemula dikirim terbang sebelum mereka bahkan bisa mendekati lima pengawal. Karena itu, semua orang di sana menyadari betapa kuatnya mereka berlima dan mereka tidak berani mendekati mereka lagi.

Lebih dari 300 Dewa Sejati telah jatuh ke Chen Feng. Selanjutnya, sinar pedang yang dia tembakkan menembus ruang dan sosoknya melintas ke depan untuk memasuki medan perang lain.

Jumlah pembudidaya di medan perang ini juga melebihi seribu. Semuanya adalah Dewa Surgawi, satu tingkat lebih tinggi dibandingkan dengan medan perang Dewa Sejati yang dia hadapi sebelumnya.

Memasuki medan perang, Chen Feng segera menjadi sasaran serangan gabungan dari puluhan Dewa Surgawi. Melihat itu, Jin Feng dan yang lainnya ingin segera bergegas ke depan.

Aku akan menangani ini. Kalian minggir. ” Chen Feng mendorong dengan kedua tangan dan perisai muncul di hadapannya. Itu tidak lain adalah Longevity Shield yang telah naik level ketingkat pseudo-Divine.

Semua serangan menyatu menjadi satu sebelum mendarat di Longevity Shield. Seketika, Chen Feng merasa seolah-olah sungai bintang dari Sembilan Surga sedang menggerogotinya. Perisai Panjang Umur hanya mampu bertahan selama satu napas waktu. Tanda Panjang Umur di permukaannya meledak secara berurutan sebelum perisai itu sendiri hancur. Aliran energi yang tersisa kemudian menjelajahi tubuh Chen Feng dan aliran cahaya berwarna-warni menyelimuti Chen Feng.

Wajah Jin Feng dan empat pengawal lainnya tenggelam dan mereka ingin bergegas maju hanya untuk dihentikan oleh puluhan pembudidaya musuh. Bahkan wajah Tie Zhen tenggelam, tetapi dua pembudidaya musuh menghentikannya.

Karena perasaan prihatin mereka, Tie Zhen dan yang lainnya berhenti menahan diri. Mereka melepaskan gelombang serangan menggelora yang dengan cepat membunuh puluhan Dewa Surgawi.

“Klan Panjang Umur! Orang-orang ini berasal dari Klan Panjang Umur! ”

Seseorang berseru dan Dewa Surgawi dari Ordo Starsource dan Sekte Pedang Abadi mengungkapkan pandangan keprihatinan.

“Jangan khawatir. Aku baik-baik saja.” Suara Chen Feng kemudian sampai ke telinga keenam pengawalnya. Setelah aliran cahaya menghilang, Chen Feng dapat dilihat di sana, sama sekali tidak terluka. Kekuatan hukum terjalin satu sama lain sebagai baju zirah tempur yang tercakup dalam berbagai rune yang melilit Chen Feng.

Armor Tempur Panjang Umur! Mata Jin Feng dan yang lainnya berbinar.

Sayangnya, ini hanya di tingkat pseudo-Divine. Konon, itu berisi kekuatan 64 hukum Surgawi. Itu seharusnya sudah sebanding dengan artefak Ilahi asli. Chen Feng menendang dengan kedua kakinya untuk menghilang dari tempatnya berdiri dan serangan yang turun dari langit meleset. Chen Feng kemudian menyerbu ke medan pertempuran yang sangat padat.

Sebelumnya, pertarungan Chen Feng melawan Dewa Sejati praktis merupakan pesta pembantaian dan bahkan Chen Feng merasa seperti pengganggu. Baru sekarang dia bisa merasa tertekan. Serangan gabungan dari puluhan Dewa Surgawi sebelumnya berhasil menghancurkan Perisai Umur Panjang dan bahkan memaksa Chen Feng mundur sedikit.

Tanpa Menara Umur Panjang untuk dijadikan asuransi dan dukungan, semuanya turun ke Chen Feng sendirian. Di hadapan tekanan, Chen Feng terus membunuh jalannya ke depan. Akhirnya, dia bisa merasakan kepuasan pertempuran.

Dari dua Dewa Surgawi tingkat tinggi yang mengepung Tie Zhen, satu terbunuh sementara yang lainnya terluka parah. Tie Zhen, di sisi lain, tidak puas dengan hasil ini. Hanya, melihat Chen Feng aman dan sehat, dia menjadi lega.

“Rekan kultivator, mengapa Pesawat Panjang Umur Surga Kamu ikut campur dalam perang antara pihak kami dan Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao? Apakah Kamu mungkin tertarik dengan air mancur spiritual di sini? Jika itu masalahnya, katakan saja dan kita akan mundur. ” Ada sedikit kekaguman dalam cara Envoy Starbreak memandang Tie Zhen.

Karena perang jutaan tahun yang lalu, kekuatan Pesawat Panjang Umur Langit agak menurun. Meski begitu, posisinya yang agung bukanlah sesuatu yang bisa dilawan oleh kekuatan seperti Starsource Order.

Keberadaan seperti Celestial Planes adalah untuk dikhawatirkan oleh Pengadilan Abadi. The Starsource Order dan Six Dao Sword Fraksi tidak mampu menahan bahkan satu jari pun dari pihak lain.

Tie Zhen mengabaikan mereka. Dengan satu langkah, dia meninggalkan medan perang ini untuk memasuki medan perang tempat Chen Feng berada. Tombak pertempuran di tangannya terayun dan puluhan Dewa Surgawi yang menghalangi jalannya semuanya terbunuh.

Tidak ada yang berani berbicara dan mengkritik Tie Zhen.

Mari kita mundur. Utusan Starbreak menghela nafas.

Chen Feng, yang sedang mengisi pertempuran di medan perang, tiba-tiba merasakan tekanan padanya berkurang. Baru kemudian dia menyadari bahwa pihak lain mundur. Melihat itu, para pembudidaya dari sisi Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao, yang sudah di ambang kekalahan, melolong karena terkejut.

Melihat sekeliling, Chen Feng segera mengerti apa yang terjadi.

Sepertinya Aku tidak bisa tinggal di Fraksi Pedang Enam Tao lagi.
