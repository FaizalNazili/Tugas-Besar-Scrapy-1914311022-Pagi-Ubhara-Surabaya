Chapter 481: Wipe Out

“My God…”

A living breathing human being had just transformed before his very eyes — how would he feel?

Lieutenant Colonel Wilson could not describe this feeling. Faced with a scene that could only appear in science fiction movies, at this moment, there was only an endless echo of “F*CK!” in his heart. The group of soldiers sent to apprehend Chen Chen could not help but stop in their tracks. Some even retreated instinctively.

Before everyone’s eyes, Chen Chen, who had turned into a huge monster, slowly stood up. As he breathed, two white streams of mist shot out of his mouth like jet valves. When Chen Chen raised his head, everyone saw that his eyes were like two large black holes and in the center of each black hole, one could even see a pupil the size of a needle tip that burned like coal fire!

Were those the pupils of a human being?

As those pupils swept across the crowd, everyone including Wilson experienced heart palpitations that seemed to come from the depths of their souls. It appeared that now they were not facing a human being at all, but a demon who had crawled out of the abyss of hell!

“What the h*ll are you…”

Finally, Lieutenant Colonel Wilson was the first one to recover. He pointed at Chen Chen again and bit the bullet. “What are you guys doing? Hurry up and apprehend him!”

Hearing Lieutenant Colonel Wilson’s urging, all the soldiers who were supposed to arrest Chen Chen were caught in a dilemma. Nonetheless, a military order must always be obeyed. Just when they were about to grit their teeth and rush forward, Chen Chen suddenly took a step, bent over, and unleashed a rumbling roar!

“Roar!”

Just like a subwoofer, Chen Chen’s roar even created an echo and the soldiers who had charged up were suddenly hit hard by the sound. Their bodies trembled and they fell straight to the ground!

“D*mn…”

Upon seeing this scene, Lieutenant Colonel Wilson’s expression changed. Although Chen Chen was extremely valuable, he also understood that he could not hold back anymore. The atmospheric pressure from a mere roar was enough to injure people. What kind of power was hidden within this devilish body?

Thinking of this, Wilson felt an irrepressible chill in his heart and he no longer hesitated to shout, “Shoot, everyone shoot now!”

At once, the dozens of soldiers at the forefront of the fortification immediately executed his order and pulled the trigger without thinking!

“Rattle rattle rattle…”

A series of gunshots resounded like thunder as endless firepower converged upon Chen Chen’s body at this moment. Then, an even more incredible scene occurred — Chen Chen was only beaten back a few steps. That armor of white bone had blocked the countless bullets, which jingled as they dropped to the ground!

The powerful 7.26 rifle bullet could not even penetrate the shell of the monster in front of them!

“Hahahahaha!!! You know nothing about true power!!!”

In the rain of bullets, Chen Chen laughed wildly, his voice booming like thunder, even overshadowing the snarl of bullets. He suddenly leaned forward and at the same time, his legs bent slightly, and then he sprang up!

“Whoosh!”

There was a sound of something tearing through the air. Everyone only saw a quick blur. Chen Chen’s huge body shot up dozens of meters into the sky!

“How is this possible with such a massive build!”

Such a thought flashed in everyone’s minds. They subconsciously looked into the sky, only to see Chen Chen already appearing above their heads, and with a boom, the front of a military truck was directly squashed by Chen Chen!

“Agh…”

The soldiers nearby screamed. They had just aimed at Chen Chen with their guns, but before they had time to fire, Chen Chen’s arms swung like a pendulum!

“Pfff pfff pfff pfff!”

The two-meter-long barbs on Chen Chen’s elbow were like two sharp sickles. In just one stroke, the soldiers in that area fell to pieces. A large amount of blood and internal organs were flung out, splashing the soldiers behind!

“Agh agh ahh ahh aghh!”

Everyone screamed like they had seen a ghost. Some soldiers no longer cared about avoiding their own teammates and started to pull the trigger frantically!

However, at this time, Chen Chen was like a giant beast in a flock of sheep. He bent his legs again and rushed into another pile of people like a bomb. Then there was another burst of limbs splattering everywhere and four or five soldiers were directly grounded into a pulp!

“Disperse, everyone, disperse immediately!”

The battle commander near the group shouted. All the soldiers screamed and scattered in all directions, but their speed was like a toddler’s compared to Chen Chen who was like an agile cheetah, rushing around in the crowd and killing as he pleased!

“Where’s the sniper? Where’s the grenade platoon? Get them to strike now!”

In the command vehicle, Wilson’s eyes were popping out. He yelled frantically, but the adjutant next to him rushed forward and yelled, “No way, Lieutenant Colonel. He’s among our men now. We don’t dare to fire!”

“Fire, let them fire boldly. We must kill this monster!”

Wilson bellowed while being heavily protected by his guards and he was herded into the command vehicle by the guards.

Wilson’s command vehicle was an infantry fighting vehicle. After seeing this horrible display by Chen Chen, the command vehicle quickly started its engine, preparing to flee into the distance.

“Will you manage to escape?”

Suddenly, after killing dozens of soldiers around him, Chen Chen turned his head. He snarled in a low voice and then suddenly exerted force from his feet, and sprang 20 or 30 meters into the sky!

“Boom!”

In the next second, Chen Chen’s huge body crashed down from a great height and he landed directly in front of the infantry fighting vehicle. His sharp teeth appeared like two sawtooth rows, and at the same time, he half-arched his body, assuming a posture of confrontation!

“Crash!”

There was a massive impact and the infantry fighting vehicle slammed into Chen Chen’s body. Suddenly Chen Chen was hit by the infantry fighting vehicle and driven backward!

However, as he found his balance, Chen Chen suddenly straightened his legs. As the infantry vehicle continued to move forward, Chen Chen’s feet plowed two deep ditches into the ground!

After plowing a ditch of more than ten meters on the road, the infantry fighting vehicle was unexpectedly forced to stop!

At the same time, Chen Chen let out a roar. His arms suddenly exploded with strength. The entire armored vehicle was lifted directly and then it was turned upside down in a raging tumult!

However, just then, there was a flash of fire on a helicopter not far away. Chen Chen had just flipped over the armored vehicle and suddenly felt a jolt in his head. He scratched his head and a deformed warhead with a caliber of more than three centimeters slid off his head and hit the ground with a loud “clank”.

This was the caliber of anti-material sniper rifles!

“Hmm?”

Chen Chen quickly turned his head and looked into the sky. He saw a helicopter hovering at an altitude of thirty to forty meters and then there was another flash of fire from the helicopter!

This time, Chen Chen tilted his head at once and a whistling sound cut by his ear. Another bullet had passed by Chen Chen’s ear and a deep hole with the thickness of a bowl was excavated right behind Chen Chen!

“Little worm, you’ve hurt me!!!”

Provoked by this opponent, Chen Chen could not help but let out a roar and then bent his legs again!

“Oh no!”

On the helicopter, the pilot saw this familiar scene. He suddenly felt a pang of horror and alarm for some reason. Without thinking about it, he started to pull up the helicopter as if he was preparing to avoid the monster’s attack!

Even so, how could the agility of a helicopter compare to that of a creature? In the next second, Chen Chen’s body bounced forth like a cannonball. At this moment, the air seemed to be torn apart, rippling with exploding airwaves!

“Bang!”

Chen Chen’s body slammed into the helicopter. The sniper in the back seat was smashed into a pulp by Chen Chen before he could even react. The airplane further lost its balance, spinning as it fell to the ground…

“Boom!”

The moment the helicopter hit the ground, a huge fireball suddenly rose, but Chen Chen rushed out of the fireball as if nothing had happened. He leaped again and returned to the crowd!

A massacre from hell seemed to have begun…

Wherever Chen Chen passed by, there were corpses all over the ground and severed limbs flying through the air. These soldiers were as fragile as clay and paper. With just a wave of Chen Chen’s hands, several lives were dispelled like the flicker of fireworks, fading away subsequently…

This group of soldiers wanted to flee, but before they got very far, they would be blocked by Chen Chen. Some of those who fled by car were shot and killed directly in the middle of the road. Finally, a few straggling soldiers escaped the Herrank Warehouse District. However, a few distorted transparent figures suddenly appeared in front of them and before they could react, their heads were separated from their bodies!

At this time, the second group of Black Knights sent by Little X had already arrived. Although their numbers were small and they could not join the fight directly, sealing the exit routes and killing any deserters was a piece of cake.

With the incredible marksmanship of the Black Knights, even those high-altitude helicopters were easily killed and one after another fell…

After about ten minutes, the periphery of the Herrank Warehouse District was full of corpses. There were exploded military vehicles and bodies everywhere and flames blazed to the sky, illuminating the fringes of the Herrank Warehouse District.

A soldier with torn legs made a low moan. While looking at a nearby distance, he pulled himself forward with his hands as if he wanted to escape the war zone. Still, in the next second, a pair of claws that was only half his size stretched out, tossed him up high, and then threw him down viciously!

“Splatter!”

There was a sound of bodily fragmentation and at the same time, the scream of the wounded soldier stopped abruptly!

In the flames, more than a dozen translucent figures emerged from the darkness. All of them were wearing black tights that covered their whole body, with strangely shaped weapons in both hands as they looked around blankly.

They walked along as if this was a battlefield in hell. Any soldier who pretended to be dead or unconscious, but who was still breathing, would be shot by them, and then they continued to search for the next life that had not yet been extinguished…

As for Chen Chen, he panted with mist emitting from his mouth while he walked slowly to an overturned tank. He bent over slightly but saw that the hatch of the infantry tank had been opened, and the tank was empty.

“Hmm?”

Chen Chen looked around with charcoal-like pupils and suddenly saw a line of bloodstains and footprints extending into the interior of the Herrank Warehouse District.

“Someone’s slipped through the net?”

Chen Chen murmured in a low voice. He tilted his head while stroking his neck and then strode into the Herrank Warehouse District with dull thudding footsteps…

At this time, on the other side, a soldier was carrying Lieutenant Colonel Wilson and constantly traveling deeper into the Herrank Warehouse District. Another soldier was in charge of guarding the rear. The three of them kept panting and wandering into the depths of the Herrank Warehouse District.

“Pant… Pant…”

Wilson only felt his lungs burning furiously. After the armored car was overturned by Chen Chen, he had hit his chest severely. At this time, he did not know how many of his ribs were broken, so he could only rely on his soldiers to flee.

At this time, he panted while looking behind him, only to see the glare of fires surging into the sky from a distance, and the sound of gunshots that were gradually becoming more sporadic. The battle was over.

“D*mn, what the h*ll was that thing? Why is it invulnerable, with power and speed beyond any lifeform on Earth!”

Wilson growled unhappily, but he was immediately advised by the soldier beside him. “Lieutenant Colonel, now isn’t the time to talk about this. I can’t contact headquarters for some reason, so the most important thing for us is to escape and explain everything to the higher-ups. Otherwise the casualties of this battle today will be covered up by Blacklight Biotechnology!”

Hearing the advice of the soldier, Wilson’s face turned even darker. He was just about to say something when suddenly, there was a loud noise behind him as if something heavy had fallen on the ground!

“Boom!”

Hearing this noise, both of their faces changed. At the same time, the soldier who was responsible for guarding the rear also ran over. “Lieutenant Colonel, that monster is coming over!”

Hearing this confirmation from the soldier, Wilson finally realized that he could not escape. He pushed away the soldier who was supporting him and shouted, “Split up and run. Only in this way will one of us have a chance to escape and expose everything that happened today!”

Hearing Wilson’s order, the two soldiers also understood that he was speaking the truth, so the three of them stopped hesitating. They immediately separated and fled in three directions!

Just ten seconds after the three of them had rushed into the darkness, a huge black shadow suddenly crashed into the ground from the sky. In the smoke and dust, the huge inhuman body slowly straightened up from the ground and looked around. Chen Chen’s keen sense of hearing suddenly picked up three sets of footsteps gradually moving away.

“Little worms, do you think you can escape by splitting up?”

Chen Chen grinned with two rows of fangs. He chuckled lowly, picked a random position, and then leaped forward formidably!

“Boom!”

Just as one of the soldiers had reached a fork in the road, Chen Chen landed directly in front of him. The huge shock wave knocked the soldier to the ground!

Seeing that his escape route was completely blocked, a glint of despair finally appeared in the soldier’s eyes. Before he got up, he raised his gun and aimed a spray of bullets at Chen Chen!

“Rattle rattle!”

There were bursts of intensive gunfire, but before even a third of the bullets had been fired, his body was smashed like a meat patty!

“Splat!”

Chen Chen slowly retracted his palm and glanced at the soldier’s body that had been reduced to only two dimensions. Then he jumped again in the other direction that he had memorized!

A few seconds later, Chen Chen looked at the other corpse that no longer resembled a human being in the palm of his hand, then threw the opponent down like trash. After that, he leaped toward the last position!

“Boom!”

This time, Chen Chen had blocked Wilson directly!

Looking at Wilson, who was backed into a wall, with his eyes wide and round, Chen Chen walked toward the other person step by step. “Run, little worm, why don’t you keep running? Could it be that you have given up?”

Listening to Chen Chen’s mocking, Wilson’s eyes flashed with a sharp glimmer. A hint of joy turned up the corners of his mouth and he laughed uncontrollably. “Hahahaha, monster, no matter what the h*ll you are, it’s all over now. Because you are dead!”

“Oh?”

When Chen Chen heard these words, he paused. His coal-like pupils moved slightly to the right. “Do you mean…”

Before Chen Chen finished speaking, Wilson, who had kept his eyes wide open, suddenly closed his eyes!

In the next second, Chen Chen suddenly felt a chill approaching behind him. Before he could turn his head, he felt a slight tingling in the back of his neck!

“Crack!”

There was a sound as if something was shattered. Wilson opened his eyes immediately after hearing this sound, but then his eyes bulged in sheer disbelief!

Behind the huge monster in front of him, there was a statue with both arms around the monster’s neck. Even so, the monster simply tugged lightly and dragged the angel statue to the front.

“Not bad, it crushed a piece of my exoskeleton.”

Chen Chen touched the split in the carapace at his nape, which then healed swiftly at a visible speed. After a second, the carapace had regained its smoothness as if the crack had just been an illusion.

After he had casually pinched the angel statue into dust, Chen Chen turned his head again to look at Wilson who was stunned. He grinned and uttered in a low voice like a brass bell, “Lieutenant Colonel Wilson, now there’s only you and me…”

Dalam kobaran api, lebih dari selusin sosok tembus pandang muncul dari kegelapan. Mereka semua mengenakan celana ketat hitam yang menutupi seluruh tubuh mereka, dengan senjata berbentuk aneh di kedua tangan saat mereka melihat sekeliling dengan hampa.

Mereka berjalan bersama seolah-olah ini adalah medan perang di neraka. Setiap prajurit yang berpura-pura mati atau tidak sadarkan diri, tetapi masih bernafas, akan ditembak oleh mereka, dan kemudian mereka terus mencari kehidupan selanjutnya yang belum padam…

Adapun Chen Chen, dia terengah-engah dengan kabut yang keluar dari mulutnya saat dia berjalan perlahan ke tangki yang terbalik. Dia membungkuk sedikit tetapi melihat bahwa palka tank infanteri telah dibuka, dan tank itu kosong.

“Hmm?”

Chen Chen melihat sekeliling dengan pupil seperti arang dan tiba-tiba melihat garis noda darah dan jejak kaki memanjang ke interior Distrik Gudang Herrank.

Seseorang menyelinap melalui jaring?

Chen Chen bergumam dengan suara rendah. Dia memiringkan kepalanya sambil membelai lehernya dan kemudian melangkah ke Distrik Gudang Herrank dengan langkah kaki berdebar tumpul…

Pada saat ini, di sisi lain, seorang tentara membawa Letnan Kolonel Wilson dan terus-menerus melakukan perjalanan lebih dalam ke Distrik Gudang Herrank. Prajurit lain bertugas menjaga bagian belakang. Ketiganya terus terengah-engah dan berkeliaran di kedalaman Distrik Gudang Herrank.

“Pant… Pant…”

Wilson hanya merasakan paru-parunya terbakar habis-habisan. Setelah mobil lapis baja itu dijungkirbalikkan oleh Chen Chen, dia telah memukul dadanya dengan parah. Saat ini, dia tidak tahu berapa banyak tulang rusuknya yang patah, jadi dia hanya bisa mengandalkan pasukannya untuk melarikan diri.

Pada saat ini, dia terengah-engah sambil melihat ke belakang, hanya untuk melihat nyala api melonjak ke langit dari kejauhan, dan suara tembakan yang secara bertahap menjadi lebih sporadis. Pertempuran telah usai.

“Sial, benda apa itu? Mengapa itu kebal, dengan kekuatan dan kecepatan melebihi makhluk hidup mana pun di Bumi! ”

Wilson menggeram tidak senang, tapi dia segera dinasehati oleh prajurit di sampingnya. Letnan Kolonel, sekarang bukan waktunya untuk membicarakan hal ini. Aku tidak dapat menghubungi kantor pusat karena suatu alasan, jadi yang terpenting bagi kami adalah melarikan diri dan menjelaskan semuanya kepada atasan. Kalau tidak, korban pertempuran hari ini akan ditutupi oleh Blacklight Biotechnology! ”

Mendengar nasihat prajurit itu, wajah Wilson menjadi semakin gelap. Dia baru saja akan mengatakan sesuatu ketika tiba-tiba, ada suara keras di belakangnya seolah-olah ada sesuatu yang berat jatuh ke tanah!

“Ledakan!”

Mendengar suara itu, wajah keduanya berubah. Di saat yang sama, prajurit yang bertugas menjaga bagian belakang juga berlari. Letnan Kolonel, monster itu akan datang!

Mendengar konfirmasi dari tentara tersebut, Wilson akhirnya sadar bahwa dia tidak bisa melarikan diri. Dia mendorong tentara yang mendukungnya dan berteriak, “Berpisah dan lari. Hanya dengan cara ini salah satu dari kita akan memiliki kesempatan untuk escape dan ungkapkan semua yang terjadi hari ini! ”

Mendengar perintah Wilson, kedua tentara itu juga mengerti bahwa dia mengatakan yang sebenarnya, jadi mereka bertiga berhenti ragu-ragu. Mereka segera berpisah dan melarikan diri ke tiga arah!

Hanya sepuluh detik setelah mereka bertiga bergegas ke kegelapan, bayangan hitam besar tiba-tiba jatuh ke tanah dari langit. Dalam asap dan debu, tubuh besar yang tidak manusiawi itu perlahan berdiri tegak dari tanah dan melihat sekeliling. Indera pendengaran Chen Chen yang tajam tiba-tiba mengambil tiga set langkah kaki yang perlahan menjauh.

“Cacing kecil, menurutmu kamu bisa kabur dengan berpisah?”

Chen Chen menyeringai dengan dua baris taring. Dia terkekeh rendah, memilih posisi acak, dan kemudian melompat ke depan dengan hebat!

“Ledakan!”

Tepat ketika salah satu tentara mencapai persimpangan jalan, Chen Chen mendarat tepat di depannya. Gelombang kejut yang sangat besar menjatuhkan prajurit itu ke tanah!

Melihat rute pelariannya benar-benar diblokir, kilatan keputusasaan akhirnya muncul di mata prajurit itu. Sebelum dia bangun, dia mengangkat senjatanya dan mengarahkan semprotan peluru ke Chen Chen!

“Rattle rattle!”

Ada semburan tembakan intensif, tetapi bahkan sebelum sepertiga dari peluru telah ditembakkan, tubuhnya hancur seperti patty daging!

Percikan!

Chen Chen perlahan menarik telapak tangannya dan melirik tubuh prajurit yang telah direduksi menjadi hanya dua dimensi. Kemudian dia melompat lagi ke arah lain yang dia ingat!

Beberapa detik kemudian, Chen Chen melihat mayat lain yang tidak lagi menyerupai manusia di telapak tangannya, lalu melemparkan lawannya seperti sampah. Setelah itu, dia melompat ke posisi terakhir!

“Ledakan!”

Kali ini, Chen Chen langsung memblokir Wilson!

Melihat Wilson, yang disandarkan ke dinding, dengan mata lebar dan bulat, Chen Chen berjalan menuju orang lain selangkah demi selangkah. “Lari, cacing kecil, kenapa kamu tidak terus berlari? Mungkinkah kamu sudah menyerah? ”

Mendengarkan ejekan Chen Chen, mata Wilson berkilat-kilat. Sedikit kegembiraan muncul di sudut mulutnya dan dia tertawa tak terkendali. “Hahahaha, monster, tidak peduli apa pun dirimu, semuanya sudah berakhir sekarang. Karena kamu sudah mati! ”

Oh?

Ketika Chen Chen mendengar kata-kata ini, dia berhenti. Muridnya yang seperti batu bara bergerak sedikit ke kanan. “Maksudmu…”

Sebelum Chen Chen selesai berbicara, Wilson, yang tetap membuka matanya lebar-lebar, tiba-tiba menutup matanya!

Detik berikutnya, Chen Chen tiba-tiba merasakan hawa dingin mendekat di belakangnya. Sebelum dia bisa menoleh, dia merasakan sedikit kesemutan di belakang lehernya!

“Retak!”

Ada suara seolah-olah ada sesuatu yang pecah. Wilson segera membuka matanya setelah mendengar suara ini, tapi kemudian matanya melotot karena tidak percaya!

Di belakang monster besar di depannya, ada patung dengan kedua tangan melingkari leher monster itu. Meski begitu, monster itu hanya menarik-nariknya dengan ringan dan menyeret patung malaikat itu ke depan.

“Tidak buruk, itu menghancurkan bagian exoskeleton Aku.”

Chen Chen menyentuh celah karapas di tengkuknya, yang kemudian sembuh dengan cepat dengan kecepatan yang terlihat. Setelah sedetik, karapas itu kembali mulus seolah retakan itu hanyalah ilusi.

Setelah dia dengan santai mencubit patung malaikat itu menjadi debu, Chen Chen menoleh lagi untuk melihat Wilson yang tertegun. Dia menyeringai dan mengucapkan dengan suara rendah seperti lonceng kuningan, “Letnan Kolonel Wilson, sekarang hanya ada Kamu dan Aku …”
