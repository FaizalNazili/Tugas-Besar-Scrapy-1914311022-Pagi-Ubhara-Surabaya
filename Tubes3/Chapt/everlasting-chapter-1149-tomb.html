As a True Immortal, by forming True laws, the cultivator in question could then develop their bodily world. By enhancing their world power, they would be able to improve their cultivation base. 

However, Chen Feng’s situation was very different compared to most other cultivators. The number of insight acupoints he chose to develop into worlds was simply too high. Most of the members of the Longevity Clan would choose to develop the Longevity acupoint into their bodily world. Occasionally, some might choose some other insight acupoints to become their second or third bodily worlds. However, there were none like Chen Feng, who would choose to develop so many insight acupoints into bodily worlds at the same time.

In comparison, the rate at which Chen Feng was developing his bodily worlds also became much slower. That said, the size of his insight acupoints – most of them – had long since reached the standard of a pocket dimension. That was despite Chen Feng not taking action to raise any living creatures there. With enough living creatures living inside, the pocket dimension would become a minor world. Eventually, following the birth of new lives from that minor world, the birth of native living creatures of the world, that world would become a major world. And so, it would become a genuine bodily world.

At that very moment, however, they were best suited to store the corpses of the divine dragons. Large amounts of draconic energy roiled within them. Refining the draconic energy would improve Chen Feng’s strength.

Speaking of which, the quality of the draconic energy was much higher than the immortal energy of the Immortal Plane. Only, it also contained death energy and draconic might. Care must be taken when refining this energy. 

And so, Chen Geng’s group broke down three tombs of divine dragons in a row, obtaining a high number of treasures and dragon corpses. However, all of them were at the Heavenly Immortal stage. That did disappoint Chen Feng somewhat. 

Along the way, Chen Feng also encountered several groups of cultivators. Chen Feng then proceeded to do the most natural thing to do, kill and plunder them naked.

“Young Master, no need to worry. There will surely be higher-levelled tombs here. It’s just that we have yet to find them.” Noticing the expression on Chen Feng’s face, Tun Ri quickly uttered words of comfort.

“Eh?” The expression on Da Feng’s face suddenly flickered before turning into a peculiar smile.

“What is it?”

“Something good just happened. Come look.”

After saying that, Da Feng stretched out a finger and jabbed forward. Next, the area of space before him shook, rippling like the surface of water before transforming into a screen. Images could be seen flashing within it. Surprisingly, it was none other than Heavenly Immortal Longevity Brightness and Prince Fire. Behind them were over 30 cultivators. Several of them were actually high-level Heavenly Immortals.

“These two sure have some skills. To think that they could hide such a force. Doesn’t this mean that their overall strength has surpassed us?” Tie Zhen said, surprised at the sight.

“Humph! They’re not the only ones with some skills.” Despite the derisive comment from Tun Ri, his face was somewhat ugly to behold.

“That’s not the most important issue here. Look what they found!” Da Feng hastily said.

The images gradually grew clear and sounds could be heard coming from the screen, albeit intermittently.

A tomb, not particularly big in size, appeared on the screen. There were some cracks on it and minute rays of golden light seeped out from within.

“The aura of half-step Gold Immortals. Unexpectedly, these two fellows are quite lucky,” Chen Feng said smilingly.

“Lucky indeed.” Tian Wu sneered. 

“Unfortunately for them, everything must go to the Young Master.”

“Let’s not be hasty here. We’ll wait. They haven’t taken action yet.”

“Longevity Brightness, what do you say? Are you really going to contact that brat?” Prince Fire said, his voice dripping with malice. 

“Longevity Celestial is the most noble Young Master of our imperial family’s bloodlines. I hope you can be more respectful in addressing him,” Longevity Brightness said.

“Cut the crap. There’s no one else here. There is no need to put on an act here.” Prince Fire scoffed.

“I am a member of the imperial family. The order I received this time is to find Young Master Longevity Celestial and obey his commands. Remember, I am a Flag Captain of the Boundless Corps. Everything I do must be for the benefit of the Boundless Corps.

“Due to that, now that I have discovered this tomb, the first thing I have to do is to immediately contact Young Master Longevity Celestial.” After saying that, Longevity Brightness brought out a talisman. 

“Once I crush this talisman, it will not take long before Young Master Longevity Celestial arrives. Although we have quite some men with us, the three Dark Soulsmen are not to be trifled with. Do not forget, Longevity Celestial has the purest imperial genes. Tsk, tsk. It allows him to innately suppress others from the Longevity Clan.” After saying that, Longevity Brightness gritted his teeth and his face turned somewhat contorted.

“Fine, you win. You get 60% of the treasures inside.” Prince Fire waved his hand. 

“Deal!”

Heavenly Immortal Longevity Brightness instantly calmed down and the Messaging Talisman in his hand disappeared with a flick of his hand. 

“If so, let’s hurry up and take action!”

Through the screen, Chen Feng’s group watched as the two sides ended the conversation and began attacking the tomb together. Instantly, draconic energy surged out, golden light flashed and various types of attacks shot out. It seemed to be quite the intense situation.

“I didn’t think that Heavenly Immortal Longevity Brightness is such a person!” Heavenly Immortal Brightmoon said with a somewhat resentful tone. They were both members of side branches of the imperial family and were thus somewhat acquainted with one another.

“It’s normal. In all of the Celestial Longevity Plane, there are only a handful who are not envious of me,” Chen Feng said.

“Let’s wait some more. When we attack, I believe that the looks of their faces must surely be spectacular,” Chen Feng continued.

“Hopefully, the other party can unleash all their strength for this. The way I see it, this tomb is no ordinary one,” Da Feng said, laughing.

“Is there a need for you to say that? This is a tomb of half-step Golden Immortals. How could it be easy to break open? Still, they have quite a lot of men on their side. Hopefully, that means they can open it faster. I am already about to lose my patience,” Tun Ri said excitedly.

“If you’re so impatient, you can go attack by yourself later,” Da Feng said with displeasure.

“Enough, stop quarrelling. This is the Graveyard of Divine Dragons. Anything can happen here. Even though they are attacking the tomb, they are also taking precautions. You fellows should think of how we can obtain the most benefits with the least effort,” Chen Feng said. At the same time, he also utilized the Divine Eyes of Heavenly Insight to scan their surroundings. 

Although there were guardians posted at the Graveyard of Divine Dragons, they could not stop over 10,000 cultivators. More, there were even some half-step Gold Immortals amongst the invaders.

Even now, Chen Feng had yet to encounter the cultivators from the Six Daos Sword Faction and the half-step Gold Immortals. He could sense that their auras were becoming increasingly faint. Sensing that, Chen Feng knew that this Graveyard of Divine Dragons was very big. 

“Young Master, it’s nearly done.” Tun Ri interrupted Chen Feng’s scanning efforts.

Looking at the screen, Chen Feng saw that they had blasted open a huge crack on the tomb. A concentrated golden light then shone out from the crack to seemingly pierce space and lure over a high number of cultivators.

“Not yet. Wait some more. If we take action now, we will still have to exert some effort for it. Now that other cultivators have arrived, Prince Fire and Longevity Brightness will naturally have to go all out to stop them.” Although Chen Feng was also feeling moved, he forcibly suppressed that feeling.

“Young Master is right. There needs to be more deaths on their side.” Da Feng nodded, expressing his agreement.  

Helpless, Tun Ri had to endure it. That said, he was already gathering his strength.

Boom!

Finally, the tomb burst apart and clumps of golden light – a high number of them – shot out. Due to that, even more cultivators were lured over and they all attempted to snatch the clumps of golden light. Longevity Brightness and Prince Fire snatched the clumps of golden light while killing off the cultivators who came. 

“Attack!”

Chen Feng’s eyes lit up and the Divine Sun Needle flashed forward, disappearing into the screen. Da Feng then swung both hands to rip apart both the screen and space there. Next, Tun Ri, Tian Wu and the rest charged into the resulting spatial crack. 

After Da Feng entered, the screen shattered to bits and the spatial crack closed up.

Pa! Pa! Pa! Pa! Pa!

The Divine Sun Needle kept flashing about. It looked as though a star had blown up and the sky was filled with dazzling light. By the time the Divine Sun Needle returned to Chen Feng’s hand, it had killed off over 10 cultivators.

Additionally, the Primary Spirit Diagram of First Origin transformed into a torrential river to sweep about. Everywhere it went, the clumps of golden light would be sucked in.

Roar!

Tun Ri had transformed into his original form – also a divine dragon – and a high number of cultivators were smashed aside by his mountainous body. By returning to his original form, Tun Ri grew several notches stronger. But that was not all. The most terrifying power of Tun Ri was his innate skill, the Sun Devourer. A massive black hole appeared from his mouth and cultivators were constantly sucked in. By then, he had utterly forgotten about snatching the treasures from the tomb. He was just blindly killing the cultivators there.

And yet, despite the display of ferocity from Tun Ri, the one with the highest kill count was Tian Wu. With every blink of the eye, hundreds of astral blades would shoot out from his Heavenly limbs. In just one breath’s worth of time, over 10,000 astral blades could be seen charging through the sky. Tian Wu was a true killing machine. It didn’t matter if it was Prince Fire, Longevity Brightness or the other cultivators. Tian Wu’s attacks targeted every enemy there.

Da Feng, on the other hand, was making use of his speed to collect the treasures that were flying out. Two humongous hurricanes kept sweeping around, devouring everything. In the end, they even broke into the tomb and collect two corpses of divine dragons inside.

Those were divine dragons at the half-step Gold Immortal stage. Seeing that, the eyes of Prince Fire and Longevity Brightness nearly bled.

“Da Feng!” Prince Fire roared and he transformed into a flaming divine bird to charge towards Da Feng.

Puchi!

Following the sound, a bloody hole appeared on Prince Fire’s body. The Divine Sun Needle could be seen jumping slightly on Chen Feng’s hand.

“Now that it’s come to this, are you still going to struggle?” A sword appeared in Da Feng’s hand. The blade of the sword was transparent, seemingly forged from the wind.

No overt movements could be seen from Da Feng, but Prince Fire abruptly froze in mid-air, a look of disbelief in his eyes.

“The Divine Shadowless Sword!”

Bang!

A bloody line appeared across Prince Fire’s body. In the end, with a banging sound, Prince Fire’s body – the body of a divine bird – split into two.

“You’re right. It is none other than the Divine Shadowless Sword.” Da Feng swung the sword a few more times and Prince Fire was utterly shredded to bits. He could no longer recover.

“He died a thorough death.” Chen Feng shook his head as he looked at the sword in Da Feng’s hand. 

A good divine sword! That was Chen Feng’s evaluation of the sword.

The burst of power that Chen Feng’s group had been gathering up managed to utterly change and pacify the situation. Earlier, the opening of the tomb had lured over 200 cultivators. Prince Fire’s group had dealt with a small portion of those cultivators while Chen Feng’s group dealt with the rest. 

Longevity Brightness was the only survivor. Beating him up, they then tied him up. As for Longevity Brightness and Prince Fire’s subordinates, all of them had been killed off. 

“You do not have the right to kill me! I am also a member of the imperial family!” Longevity Brightness bellowed, his usual demeanour of calmness and dashing looks nowhere to be found.

Mengaum!

Dengan setiap kedipan mata, ratusan bilah astral akan keluar dari anggota tubuh Surgawi-nya. Hanya dalam waktu satu tarikan napas, lebih dari 10.000 bilah astral dapat terlihat menerobos langit. “Da Feng!” Pangeran Api meraung dan dia berubah menjadi burung suci yang menyala untuk menyerang Da Feng.

“Sekarang sudah begini, apakah kamu masih akan berjuang?” Sebuah pedang muncul di tangan Da Feng. Bilah pedang itu transparan, sepertinya ditempa dari angin.

Tidak ada gerakan terang-terangan yang bisa dilihat dari Da Feng, tetapi Pangeran Api tiba-tiba membeku di udara, tatapan tidak percaya di matanya.

Pedang Tanpa Bayangan Ilahi!

Bang!

Garis berdarah muncul di seluruh tubuh Pangeran Api. Pada akhirnya, dengan suara benturan, tubuh Pangeran Api – tubuh burung dewa – terbelah menjadi dua.

Dia meninggal dengan kematian yang menyeluruh. Chen Feng menggelengkan kepalanya saat dia melihat pedang di tangan Da Feng.

Pedang ilahi yang bagus! Itu adalah evaluasi pedang Chen Feng.

Ledakan kekuatan yang telah dikumpulkan kelompok Chen Feng berhasil benar-benar mengubah dan menenangkan situasi. Sebelumnya, pembukaan makam telah memikat lebih dari 200 pembudidaya. Kelompok Pangeran Api telah menangani sebagian kecil dari para pembudidaya itu sementara kelompok Chen Feng menangani sisanya.

Longevity Brightness adalah satu-satunya yang selamat. Memukuli dia, mereka kemudian mengikatnya. Adapun Longevity Brightness dan bawahan Prince Fire, semuanya telah terbunuh.

“Kamu tidak berhak membunuhku! Aku juga anggota keluarga kekaisaran! ” Longevity Brightness berteriak, sikap tenang dan gagahnya yang biasa terlihat tidak bisa ditemukan.
